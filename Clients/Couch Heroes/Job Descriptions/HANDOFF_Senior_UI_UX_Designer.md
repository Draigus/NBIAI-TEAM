## Key decisions

- **Title:** Senior UI/UX Designer (upgraded from mid-level after discussion with Glen)
- **Reports to:** Game Director (Glen's direction, not Art Director as initially assumed)
- **Full-pipeline role:** This person designs, creates art assets, AND builds in UMG. One discipline, not three. Glen was emphatic about this.
- **Subsection structure:** Two H3s under "Your Role": Interface Design and Production, Collaboration and Validation. Earlier drafts had 3-4 subsections that artificially separated design/art/code as if they were different jobs. Glen corrected this.
- **Experience:** 5+ years, shipped title with in-engine UI ownership
- **No em dashes** anywhere in the document (Glen's standing rule)
- **No specific game name-drops** (WoW, FFXIV, etc.) in the must-haves. Toned down to "familiarity with MMO interface conventions"
- **No art-direction specifics** (parchment, rune motifs, holographics). Kept to "work collaboratively with the Art Director to establish the visual language"
- **About Couch Heroes boilerplate** is the longer version matching the majority of JDs (not the shorter HR Operations version)
