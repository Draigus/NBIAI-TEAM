## Slide 2

Executive Summary
**Critical Takeaways:**

**Four critical takeaways  |  March 9–30, 2026**

The community broadly loves GOALS' identity: fast-paced, responsive, fair F2P model. Input responsiveness scored 3.90/5 (76% positive) and 76% of survey respondents gave a recommendation score of 7 or higher. However, three core gameplay systems — Defensive AI, Passing, and Goalkeeper balance — are the non-negotiable blockers before launch. Defensive AI scored only 2.54/5 (50.9% negative), the lowest rating of any metric in the survey.
**Passing is the number 1 reported issue across all data sources:**

"Passing" appeared in 203 out of 509 survey disappointment responses. It generated the most sustained debate in the feedback channel (Mar 9–30), was cited in 35+ suggestions with reactions, and featured in 76 Accord citations under Complaints & Frustration.
Players are not asking for manual passing; they want the AI-assisted system to honour their intent reliably. The alpha's feel was repeatedly cited as the gold standard.
**Community wants social & multiplayer modes:**

"Pro Clubs / 5v5 Ranked / 2v2" was the single most upvoted suggestion. (27 reactions) and appeared independently in 80+ survey responses. 5v5 Quickplay is already the 3rd most popular primary mode (14%) despite being early-stage. This represents the game's biggest growth lever.
**P2W is the community’s red line:**

Across all data sources, the phrase "pay to win" is by far the most cited reason players say they would quit. Players are enthusiastic supporters of the F2P model and the aging/unique player system; but any move toward direct purchase of competitive packs would trigger immediate churn. This is a retention risk that must be communicated proactively at launch.

**Recommendations — Short-Term Fixes**

Pre-launch priority matrix  |  Address before launch day
**🔴 SHIP BLOCKER**

**Defensive AI — CBs split, defenders abandon position**

Dedicated sprint · Restore CB cover-shadow logic · Ensure midfielders hold shapeCommunicate changes explicitly in patch notes  (50.9% neg, 94 Accord citations)
**🔴 SHIP BLOCKER**

**Passing direction & target selection**

Increase player-intent weight in pass target algorithm · Direction > nearest-player snapRestore alpha pass feel · Consider Passing Assistance Level 0–2 slider  (76 citations)
**🔴 SHIP BLOCKER**

Game freeze / crash on launch (PC + consoles) [HEAVY IN THE BEGINNING OF THE BETA, REPORTS ARE FROM THAT PERIOD]
Stability pass on startup sequence · Add 'stuck' timeout with auto-retryExpand server region auto-detection  (15% of all bug reports)
**🟡 HIGH**

**Goalkeeper balance (rush too dominant in 1v1) [After the update in GKs]**

Balance GK rush hitbox / speed · Improve chip shot as valid counterFinesse shot restoration pass  (74 survey + 22 Accord citations)
**🟡 HIGH**

**Ping spike mid-match + cross-region routing**

Add max-ping threshold in matchmaking settingsCommunicate server coverage roadmap to community  (multiple threads)
**🟡 HIGH**

**"Score 10 Goals" reward blocked by early quitters**

Count goals past 70th min regardless of forfeit — OR add solo-challenge alternative route(Accord insight spike: opponent quits block ranked objective)
**🔵 MEDIUM**

**PS vs Xbox button layout for PC users**

Add controller type selector in settings (PlayStation / Xbox)(8+ survey mentions, one dedicated bug category)
**🔵 MEDIUM**

**Player indicator on minimap while defending**

Highlight controlled player with distinct colour on minimap(6+ upvoted suggestion — basic QoL with high visibility impact)


## Slide 11

**Recommendations — Long-Term Roadmap**

Feature additions that will drive retention and growth
**TIER 1 — BIGGEST RETENTION DRIVERS  (Q1–Q2 post-launch)**

**5v5 Ranked Queue — Solo / Duo / Full Stack**

22↑ + 40+ survey · 14% already play 5v5 despite friction. Ranked ladder + weekly rewards + Top 500 board: the esport pathway.
**2v2 Ranked Mode**

7↑ + 25+ survey · Natural stepping stone to 5v5. Combined squad, lower barrier, strong social hook for duos.
**Pro Clubs / My Position Mode**

#1 most upvoted — 27 reactions + 80+ survey mentions. Each player locks to one position. Create Clubs, register teams. GOALS' 5v5 is the natural foundation for it.
**TIER 2 — COMMUNITY TRUST & ECONOMY  (Q2–Q3 post-launch)**

**In-Game Clans / Club System**

Tribal identity and retention hooks. Repeatedly cited as top missing social feature.
**Player Market (price-capped)**

12↑ + 30+ survey. Cautious community — design with price caps to avoid P2W perception.
**Draft / Capped Team Events**

15+ survey. F2P equalizer events. Reduces new-player frustration. Pack Battles concept also widely requested.
**TIER 3 — CONTENT & DISCOVERY  (Q3–Q4 post-launch)**

**Licensed Players / Legends / Icons**

25+ survey wishlist. Messi, Ronaldinho, Maradona, Zidane, Cruyff most mentioned. Content creators (NickRTFM, MaRso, KSI) also requested.
**Career Mode (Player or Manager)**

40+ survey mentions. Offline/solo audience not currently served. Huge addressable market expansion.
**Replay System + Highlights Sharing**

9 Accord + 4↑ suggestion. Critical content virality tool — players need easy clip-to-social for organic growth.
