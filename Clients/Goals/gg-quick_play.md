## Page 1

Quick Play
In the Quick Play game mode, users can play casual games against their friends(s) or other
users. The Quick Play game mode can be played in both the 1v1 and 5v5 formats. For a Quick
Play game of Classic, two users are required, one on each team. For a Quick Play game of 5v5,
any number of users between 2-10 is acceptable. 
When choosing a Quick Play 1v1 game, the user can choose between inviting a friend as their
opponent or searching for a game against a random opponent.
When choosing a Quick Play 5v5 game, a lobby interface is opened. The lobby creator can
invite the desired number of friends to the lobby. Any user in the lobby can invite people from
their friends list (code for September MVP). Lobby participants can choose to fill either one
team and search for a game against a random team with the same number of users (not MVP).
Lobby participants can also choose to fill both teams and will then play internally against each
other as the teams are set up.
