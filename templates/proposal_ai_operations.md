NBI operates differently from traditional advisory firms. We have built an AI operations layer that gives our 7-person team the analytical depth of a firm three to four times our size: persistent institutional knowledge, domain-specific intelligence pipelines, automated quality loops, and structured workflows with human checkpoints at every decision point. This is not a slide deck. It is the operational infrastructure we run our business on every day.

When we embed with your studio, we do not just advise. We build the same capability for you. Frameworks, knowledge bases, quality standards, and automated workflows that persist beyond any individual engagement. Knowledge compounds instead of walking out the door when a contractor finishes or a key hire moves on.

We have refined this approach across live-service studios, from fractional C-level engagements to embedded analytics teams. It works because it is built by people who ship games, not by management consultants who have read about them. Every workflow, every knowledge structure, and every quality check is calibrated to how game studios actually operate: fast iteration, cross-functional dependencies, and the relentless pressure of live service cadences.

## What We Build For You

- **Studio Brain** — your institutional knowledge, structured and persistent. Game design philosophy, technical architecture decisions, market positioning, team preferences, competitive intelligence. Compiled from your existing documents, meetings, and tribal knowledge. Provenance-tagged so every fact traces back to its source. Survives turnover.
- **Domain knowledge bases** — what your economy designer knows about sink/faucet balance, what your live ops team assumes about event cadence, what production has decided about milestone gates. Captured, structured, and searchable. Not buried in Confluence pages or lost in Slack threads.
- **Automated workflows** — repetitive processes (competitive monitoring, metrics reviews, sprint reporting, post-mortem analysis) handled systematically with human checkpoints at decision points. The AI handles volume and consistency. Your team handles judgement.
- **Quality loops** — documents, design specs, pricing models, and analyses scored against explicit criteria and improved iteratively before they reach stakeholders. Measurably better output, every time.
- **Continuous intelligence** *(optional retainer)* — always-on market monitoring, weekly briefings, anomaly detection, and performance tracking. NBI's human consultants available for strategic judgement calls. The system compounds: every month it knows more about your game, your market, and your competitive position.

## How It Works

**1. Audit.** We map your current knowledge landscape: what is documented, what is tribal, what is at risk of leaving with the next departure. We identify the workflows where AI leverage has the highest payoff and the knowledge gaps that create the most operational risk. This is typically a 2-3 week engagement involving document review, team interviews, and workflow observation.

**2. Build.** We architect and populate the system: Brain structure tailored to your studio, domain knowledge bases drawn from your existing documents and team expertise, workflow definitions for your highest-value processes, quality criteria calibrated to your standards. Everything is built for your specific genre, platform, development stage, and team structure. A mobile F2P studio gets different architecture from a premium PC studio.

**3. Transfer.** We train your team to use and maintain the system. The architecture is yours. Documentation is thorough. Your team can operate, extend, and evolve the system independently. NBI remains available for strategic oversight, system tuning, and continuous intelligence if desired, but the goal is capability, not dependency.
