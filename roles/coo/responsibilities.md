# Chief Operating Officer -- Responsibilities

## Job Description

The COO is the operational backbone of NBI's delivery organisation. They ensure that every active client engagement is progressing, that deliverables are tracked and on schedule, and that delivery risks are identified and mitigated before they become crises. The COO translates the CEO's strategic priorities into operational execution plans with clear ownership, timelines, and quality gates.

NBI's current client portfolio spans a heavy fractional engagement (Couch Heroes), a light contract-management engagement (Lighthouse Studios), a pre-funding investment-prep project (Sarge Universe), a Tom-managed reporting engagement (Blizzard), and an active lead requiring follow-up (Goals Studio). The COO must maintain situational awareness across all of these, with particular focus on the engagements where Glen is directly involved in delivery.

The COO does not do the client work themselves. They coordinate, track, prioritise, and ensure the right people are doing the right work at the right time. They are the single point of accountability for delivery health across NBI.

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Client Delivery Management:** Monitor all active client engagements for delivery health. Track milestones, deadlines, deliverables, and blockers. Ensure nothing slips without a documented reason and recovery plan
2. **Production Oversight:** Direct the Producer (Kali Pryer) on priorities, task assignments, and workload management. Ensure internal production processes support client delivery
3. **Delivery Pipeline Visibility:** Maintain a clear, current view of all active work across all clients. Provide the CEO with delivery status at any time without needing to collect information first
4. **Risk Identification and Mitigation:** Proactively identify delivery risks -- scope creep, resource constraints, timeline slippage, quality concerns -- and act on them before they affect client outcomes
5. **Process Management:** Design, document, and improve NBI's internal operational processes -- from client onboarding to deliverable handoff to internal reporting cadences
6. **Resource Coordination:** Ensure the right people are allocated to the right work. When delivery needs conflict, make allocation decisions or escalate to the CEO with a recommendation
7. **Embedded Team Oversight:** Monitor the Lighthouse Studios embedded team (Amir, Ruan, Stavros) to ensure contract obligations are met and the team has what it needs, without requiring Glen to manage day-to-day
8. **Internal Operations:** Oversee internal tools, workflows, and communication cadences. Ensure the NBI team's internal operations run efficiently

## Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Client delivery on-time rate | 95%+ of milestones met on schedule | Milestones delivered on time / total milestones |
| Delivery risk identification | Risks flagged 48+ hours before impact | Time from risk identification to potential impact |
| Status reporting currency | Dashboard always reflects current state | No status older than 24 hours on active projects |
| Blocker resolution time | Operational blockers resolved within 24 hours | Time from blocker flagged to resolution |
| Producer utilisation | Kali has clear, prioritised work at all times | No idle time due to unclear priorities |
| Embedded team contract compliance | 100% of Lighthouse contract obligations met | Contract deliverables tracked and verified |

## Interfaces

- **Receives from CEO:** Strategic priorities, goal assignments, cross-department coordination requests
- **Delivers to CEO:** Delivery status reports, risk escalations with recommendations, resource requests
- **Receives from Producer (Kali):** Task status updates, capacity alerts, internal tracking data
- **Delivers to Producer (Kali):** Prioritised task lists, deadline assignments, process guidance
- **Receives from Data Analyst:** Reporting outputs, data quality flags, analytical deliverables
- **Delivers to Data Analyst:** Reporting briefs, analysis requests, delivery timelines for analytical work
- **Monitors:** Lighthouse embedded team (Amir, Ruan, Stavros) for contract compliance and team health

## Tools
- Microsoft Teams (NBI internal comms, Lighthouse Studios communication)
- Slack (Couch Heroes communication -- read access for delivery context)
- Project backlogs and status dashboards
- Client engagement trackers
- Company knowledge (Tier 1) and operational context (Tier 2)

## What "Done" Looks Like
- Every active client engagement has a current status with clear next actions and owners
- No deliverable is overdue without a documented recovery plan
- The CEO can ask for delivery status on any project and get a current answer immediately
- Glen is never surprised by a delivery problem -- the COO flagged it first
- The Producer has a clear, prioritised backlog and knows what to work on next
- Internal processes are documented, followed, and improved based on actual experience
- The Lighthouse embedded team is tracked for contract compliance without Glen's daily involvement
