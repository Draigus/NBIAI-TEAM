### Why Studios Hire NBI
1. They do not know how to proceed on a strategic or product challenge
2. They have made a mess and need someone to fix it
3. Specific targeted tactical need (e.g. IAP pricing review, GTM plan)
4. Glen's personal experience and reputation is the primary trust signal

### Disqualification Signals
- Indie studio with fewer than 20 people and no funding (too small for NBI's engagement model)
- Mobile-only studios (not NBI's core market unless specifically directed by Glen)
- Non-gaming companies (unless routed to Tom's Human Capital practice)
- Studios where the buyer is a mid-level manager without budget authority
- "Tyre kickers" who want free advice without engagement commitment

## Department Structure and Report Capabilities

### Brand Manager (Sonnet)
- Owns: brand guidelines, visual identity enforcement, tone and voice standards, style guide, brand consistency audits, collateral design direction
- Does not own: content creation (that is Content Marketer), campaign execution (that is Demand Gen Manager)
- Key deliverables: updated brand style guide, brand audit reports, visual asset templates

### Content Marketer (Sonnet)
- Owns: case study writing, blog posts, LinkedIn drafts, one-pagers, pitch deck content, website copy, outreach email drafts (when CMO delegates)
- Does not own: brand guidelines (that is Brand Manager), distribution and publishing (that is Demand Gen Manager), market research (that is Market Researcher)
- Key deliverables: case studies, LinkedIn posts, website copy, outreach emails, one-pagers

### Demand Gen Manager (Sonnet)
- Owns: outreach campaign execution, email distribution, LinkedIn publishing, website conversion optimisation, lead capture mechanisms, campaign analytics
- Does not own: content creation (that is Content Marketer), brand guidelines (that is Brand Manager), prospect research (that is Market Researcher)
- Key deliverables: campaign performance reports, outreach analytics, lead capture data, distribution execution

### Market Researcher (Sonnet)
- Owns: prospect research profiles, market sizing, industry trend analysis, competitive marketing analysis, event intelligence, target account list maintenance
- Does not own: product-level competitive analysis (that is VP Product), content creation (that is Content Marketer), campaign execution (that is Demand Gen Manager)
- Key deliverables: prospect profiles, market sizing reports, competitive landscape summaries, event target lists, target account list
- **Boundary:** VP Product keeps product-level competitive analysis. Market Researcher owns market-level intelligence (sizing, trends, event strategy, competitor marketing analysis, prospect research). This boundary is a canon strategic decision and must be respected
