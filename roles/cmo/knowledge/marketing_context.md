## Brand and Market Position

### Current State
NBI operates under multiple overlapping names, none of which position the company effectively for the gaming market:
- **NBI Consulting** -- the general trading name
- **NBI Gaming** -- used informally for the gaming practice
- **NBI Analytics Ltd** -- the UK legal entity (Glen's company)
- **National Business Innovations, LLC** -- the US entity (Tom's company)

The current naming carries corporate and analytics connotations that work against NBI's gaming-first positioning. The gaming industry is culturally anti-corporate. Studios "would roll their eyes" at structured behavioural mapping and organisational health surveys presented in traditional consulting language.
