## Key Financial Risks

1. **Near break-even position:** Current contracted revenue (~£650K) barely covers current payroll (~£625K). There is essentially no margin for operational costs, tools, or unexpected expenses. Any revenue loss would immediately create a deficit
2. **NSI transition timing uncertainty:** The ~£620K/year in additional payroll has no confirmed timeline. If NSI closes suddenly, NBI would need to absorb these costs before revenue has scaled to cover them
3. **Revenue concentration:** Two clients (Lighthouse and Couch Heroes) account for virtually all contracted revenue. Losing either would be catastrophic
4. **Human Capital practice generating zero revenue:** Tom's practice has not produced contracts in any industry. His payroll cost (~£200K when transitioning from NSI) currently has no revenue to offset it
5. **Glen dependency:** Glen is the primary revenue generator and relationship holder. His capacity is the binding constraint on revenue growth
6. **Unconfirmed Blizzard revenue:** The Blizzard engagement revenue has not been confirmed, making total revenue uncertain
7. **Pipeline leads not converting:** Goals Studio follow-up is overdue. Other pipeline leads are early-stage. The gap to £1.2M requires significant new business that is not yet in the pipeline

## Financial Planning Priorities

1. **Close the 2026 revenue gap:** ~£550K needed from new business. Track pipeline conversion aggressively
2. **Model NSI transition scenarios:** Maintain current models showing when the transition becomes sustainable
3. **Confirm Blizzard revenue:** Get the actual figure from Tom so total revenue picture is complete
4. **Track Sarge Universe financial upside:** If the £10M raise succeeds, this could be a transformational engagement for NBI. Model the financial impact
5. **Assess Playsage product revenue potential:** When Playsage development resumes, model the product revenue timeline against the consulting revenue base
6. **Build project profitability discipline:** Every engagement should have a clear margin view. Unpaid work (Sarge Universe) must be tracked as an investment with expected return
