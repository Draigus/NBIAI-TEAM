## What Makes a Game Pitch Deck Work

**Process over single hit:** Voodoo, Homa, and Play Ventures all converge on this principle. Investors fund the systematised hit-generation machine, not individual games [source: web_2026-05-25_voodoo-hypercasual-fundraising-strategy].

**Industry framing + team unfair advantage:** a16z's LP deck positioned games as experiencing a "Marvel Moment" with cultural crossover, then proved thesis with team hires from Supercell, YouTube, and Riot [source: web_2026-05-25_a16z-games-fund-one-deck].

**Less is more:** Homa raised $50M with 13 slides. Their progression from 13 slides ($50M Series A) to 19 slides ($100M Series B) suggests decks expand as traction accumulates, not as aspiration grows [source: web_2026-05-25_homa-games-series-a-deck].

**Pitch the platform/process, not the game:** Homa positioned as a "game technology lab" combining creativity, data, and no-code tools. This is the Play Ventures/Katkoff principle at scale [source: web_2026-05-25_homa-games-series-a-deck].

**Concrete success metrics:** Voodoo's investor bar: D1 retention >55%, D7 >22%, CPI <$0.25. Knowing the bar matters even if most studios cannot hit it [source: web_2026-05-25_voodoo-hypercasual-fundraising-strategy].

## By Platform

### Mobile (F2P, Hypercasual, Mid-core)

**Telegram-specific economics (Sarge Universe):** CPI 7-12 cents (vs $2+ on iOS/Android), no 30% platform overhead. Target raise USD 1-2M ("goldilocks zone"), multiple USD 1M investors preferred over single large cheque. Max equity ceiling 30% [source: granola_beef5f26].

**The Games Fund template:** Published by the #1 early-stage gaming VC. Includes Google Slides pitch deck template, P&L spreadsheet, and budget calculator. The template URL is publicly accessible [source: web_2026-05-25_games-fund-vc-pitch-template].

**Voodoo benchmarks:** At 2019 peak: EUR 360M revenue, 300M MAU, 3.7B downloads, 100+ games, 250 employees, profitable. Revenue split: ~67% publishing, ~33% internal development [source: web_2026-05-25_voodoo-hypercasual-fundraising-strategy].

### PC/Console Indie (Premium, Early Access)

**The funding dead zone:** $500K-$3M range is the hardest category to fund. Little Polygon pitched 50+ publishers, gave ~24 pitches at DICE and GDC, zero term sheets. Publishers reported places previously funding $5M deals now cap at $500K-$1.5M [source: web_2026-05-25_little-polygon-pitching-postmortem].

**30 slides is too many.** Most successful decks are 10-15 slides. Little Polygon's 30-slide deck took 2+ months to build, suggesting over-engineering [source: web_2026-05-25_little-polygon-pitching-postmortem].

**Conference pitching ROI:** GDC generates the most meetings, but conversion rates are extremely low. Out-of-pocket cost for pitching: ~$10K before any development funding [source: web_2026-05-25_little-polygon-pitching-postmortem].

### Live Service / GaaS

**Attribute-offset cosmetics require explicit fairness boundaries:** If skins have plus-minus attribute offsets (run faster but less accurate), explicit disclosure rules, audit checks, and A/B testing governance are mandatory to avoid pay-to-win perception [source: chatgpt_68e3cfc8].

## By Stage

### Pre-Seed / Concept

Team, thesis, prototype, prove fun. At this stage, the deck must sell the founding team's unfair advantage and the market thesis [source: chatgpt_68ee2760].

### Seed / Pre-Production

Product-market fit evidence, soft launch metrics trending. Playsage seed ask: GBP 2.5M for 24-month runway. 14-slide structure: Title, Problem, Opportunity, Solution, Product Modules, Differentiation, Data/Tech Moat, Market Context, Buyer/JTBD, Competitive Landscape, Business Model, Traction/Roadmap, GTM, Team, The Ask [source: chatgpt_68e8a242].

### Series A / Production

Proven traction data. Homa raised $50M with 1 billion+ downloads as evidence. Grand Games raised $70M with top-2 US iOS charts and 50M downloads [source: web_2026-05-25_homa-games-series-a-deck].

### Late-Stage / Live Service

Institutional scaling. Take-Two/Zynga: FY2026 mobile net bookings $3.3B, 49% of total platform split [source: web_2026-05-25b_take-two-zynga-mobile-turnaround].

## Exemplars

### a16z Games Fund One ($660M LP Raise)

Investor: LP-facing (VC pitching to own investors). Key structural elements: industry framing ("Marvel Moment"), historical thesis ("games are killer app for new tech"), operating team from tier-1 studios. 80% of 2023 investments featured major AI components. Pivoted from Web3 to AI within one year [source: web_2026-05-25_a16z-games-fund-one-deck].

### Homa Games ($50M Series A, $100M Series B)

Paris-based mobile gaming platform. 13-slide deck for $50M raise. Pitched the platform/process, not individual games. Total funding exceeded $150M. Lead: Northzone [source: web_2026-05-25_homa-games-series-a-deck].

### Voodoo (Goldman Sachs $200M, Tencent $1.4B valuation)

Category creator (hypercasual). All European VCs passed; Goldman Sachs invested $200M. Systematised hit-generation with weekly prototypes and data-driven kill/scale decisions. Post-2020 challenges: IDFA, revenue share compression, zero consumer brand [source: web_2026-05-25_voodoo-hypercasual-fundraising-strategy].

### Sarge Universe (Active, USD 1-2M Target)

Telegram strategy game. Materials span full investment lifecycle: pitch decks, AERM forecast, GDD, legal docs, VDR checklist, 1,350-entry investor list. CPI 7-12 cents on Telegram. GDC dinner contacts include SpyFox (David/Riley Ettery), RMT (Jackson), Behold (Binnie) [source: granola_beef5f26, sarge_telegram_export_2026-05-17].

## Investor Expectations by Type

**Gaming-specialist VCs (The Games Fund, LVP, Play Ventures, BITKRAFT):** Want game-specific metrics (D1/D7/D30 retention, CPI, ARPDAU, LTV). Round definitions are games-specific: Seed = team + thesis + prototype, Series A = soft launch metrics trending [source: chatgpt_68ee2760].

**Generalist VCs (a16z, Accel, Goldman Sachs):** Need industry framing (why games, why now), cultural crossover argument, and team with named operators from elite studios [source: web_2026-05-25_a16z-games-fund-one-deck].

**Corporate/Strategic (Tencent, Savvy Games Group, Sony Ventures):** Focus on strategic fit, IP portfolio, and distribution synergies [source: chatgpt_6866b4be].

**Key investor database entries:** LVP (UK seed, GBP 500k-2M, prefers warm introductions), Konvoy (US, platforms/infra), Play Ventures (Singapore, mobile F2P), BITKRAFT (US, seed-B), Hiro Capital (EU, games/metaverse), Makers Fund (global, USD 500M fund), Griffin Gaming (US, all stages GBP 100k-100M), Savvy Games Group (Saudi, USD 533M for early-stage) [source: chatgpt_6866b4be, chatgpt_6884c9b6].

## Data Room Essentials

**16-section structure:** Company overview, cap table, team bios, market/strategy, portfolio/pipeline, game design docs, tech, platform, traction (split Premium vs F2P), monetisation, financials, legal (IP chain, middleware, GDPR), contracts, operations, references, data room hygiene [source: chatgpt_68ee2760].

**Data room audit methodology:** Seven-role investor committee simulation. Step 0: create index. Step 1: label every claim (Fact/Assumption/Projection/Opinion). Step 2: cross-document reconciliation. "Diligence traps" called out separately from general issues [source: chatgpt_6964dcca].

**Common red flags:** IP ownership chain missing, middleware licences undocumented, contractor IP assignments absent, ambiguous KPI definitions (what counts as MAU), valuing on gross receipts while collecting net [source: chatgpt_68ee2760, chatgpt_6908ac7d].

## Anti-Patterns

- **The funding dead zone:** $500K-$3M is extremely difficult to raise. Little Polygon's experience: 50+ pitches, zero funding [source: web_2026-05-25_little-polygon-pitching-postmortem]
- **VC for most game studios:** Midsummer Studios (XCOM designer Jake Solomon): $6M raised, $2M bridge, closed without shipping. VC demands "transformational" games but game studio exit events are rare [source: web_2026-05-25b_midsummer-studios-closure]
- **Single-hit studio risk:** Metacore (Merge Mansion): EUR 180M invested, then full Supercell acquisition when growth plateaued and follow-up titles failed [source: web_2026-05-25_supercell-metacore-acquisition]
- **Cherry-picked comps:** Valuation comps must show range and quartile position, never single comparators [source: chatgpt_6908ac7d]
