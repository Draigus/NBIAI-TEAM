# Competitor Watch

## Executive Summary

This bank maps NBI's competitive landscape across two dimensions: consulting competitors (firms and solo practitioners offering gaming advisory services) and PlaySage product competitors (data/analytics platforms serving the games industry). The consulting landscape is fragmented -- dominated by one scaled firm (Naavik) and dozens of solo practitioners. No competitor covers NBI's full service stack (strategic advisory + production consulting + AI operations + people/HR). The PlaySage competitor matrix identifies specific product gaps NBI can exploit. All data current as of May-June 2026.

---

## Primary Consulting Competitors

### Naavik -- Closest Direct Competitor

Founded 2019, Delaware. Three-pillar gaming advisory firm serving 300+ companies including Ubisoft, Square Enix, Meta, Epic Games, and Activision Blizzard.

**Service stack:** Strategy consulting (market research, corporate strategy, game/economy design, gamification, live ops, AI strategy, product management, brand/performance marketing). M&A and investment advisory (buy-side/sell-side, target identification, valuation, fractional CFO/CSO -- fixed-fee model to "remove conflict of interest"). Fractional talent (30+ named consultants across 8 categories: data science, engineering, game design, marketing, narrative, production, QA, visual/audio; 3-month trial, 6-month minimum).

**Team pedigrees cited:** Zynga, EA, Disney, Gameloft, King, Rovio, Wooga. Claim: 250+ startups, game studios, and financial institutions served.

**NBI vs Naavik gaps:**
- Naavik does NOT offer production consulting, studio operations, or HR/people advisory -- NBI covers all three
- Naavik's fractional talent service is a significant differentiator NBI does not currently offer
- Naavik is US-based and VC-ecosystem oriented; NBI is UK-based with deeper European and MENA client relationships
- Naavik has strong content marketing (daily digest, podcast, deep dives) as lead generation -- NBI does not replicate this
- Naavik's M&A fixed-fee model signals market preference against traditional banking fees
- No published pricing from Naavik

**Strategic monitoring:** Watch for Naavik adding production/ops consulting. NBI's AI operations advisory (EAD framework) is a defensible differentiator Naavik lacks. [source: web_2026-05-29_naavik-full-stack-gaming-consultancy]

### GameBiz Consulting -- Mobile-First Boutique

Founded 2018, Serbia. Positions as boutique consulting for video games. Claims: 80+ studios, 100+ game launches supported, EUR 2B aggregate client revenue, EUR 250M generated "directly through their monetisation strategies."

**Services:** Strategy, scaling, growth, leadership guidance for developers. Licensing, sponsorship, market entry for brands/rights holders. South-East Europe regional specialism. Mobile publishing services (BD, UA, ASO, ad monetisation). Revenue share, flat fee, or hybrid pricing.

**Named clients:** Wargaming, Pixel Federation, Nanobit, HubX, Creative Mobile, others.

**NBI positioning:** Not a strategic threat -- different service focus (tactical mobile monetisation vs strategic advisory). Revenue share pricing model is worth evaluating for NBI's pre-revenue studio engagements. Useful as referral partner for mobile-specific work. [source: web_2026-05-29_gamebiz-consulting-mobile-advisory]

## Solo Practitioner Competitors

### F2P Games Consulting (Nick Murray)

Solo practitioner offering game design, economy design, product management, live ops, monetisation strategy, due diligence, and workshops. Clients include Rovio, Nordisk Games, Goodgame Studios, Nitro Games, Cherrypick Games, 10+ others. Free initial consultation. No published pricing. [source: web_2026-05-29_boutique-f2p-economy-consultancies]

### Game Economist Consulting (Phillip Black)

Solo practitioner offering economic design, live game economy assessments ("State-of-the-Union"), analytics/product support. Three engagement models: Discovery Sprint (32 hours), One-Shot (fixed deliverables), Retainer. Covers mobile/HD, blockchain economies, non-gaming gamification. No named clients. Also runs "The Game Economist Project" connecting academic economists to game companies. [source: web_2026-05-29_boutique-f2p-economy-consultancies]

### Market Structure Observation

The game economy consulting space is dominated by solo practitioners, not firms. No one has scaled game economy consulting into a proper firm -- either the TAM is too small or the expertise is too rare to hire. Solo practitioners have limited capacity and no bench depth. NBI's embedded game economy expertise within a broader advisory offering is more valuable than isolated economy specialism. Rovio appears as a client across multiple consultancies, indicating studios shop across consultants rather than committing to one firm. [source: web_2026-05-29_boutique-f2p-economy-consultancies]

## Ecosystem Players (Not Direct Competitors)

### GameFounders -- Accelerator/Incubator

Founded 2012, Estonia. Gaming accelerator across three regions: Europe (28 teams), Southeast Asia (39 + 40 LITE), Middle East/Saudi Arabia (21 + 100 incubator, with Saudi MCIT partnership). Accelerator model (equity stakes 5-10%) is fundamentally different from paid consulting. Their Saudi programme indicates government willingness to fund gaming ecosystem development -- potential pipeline for NBI advisory with Saudi-incubated studios. Studios graduating GameFounders programmes may need NBI-level strategic advisory. [source: web_2026-05-29_gamefounders-accelerator-ecosystem]

## PlaySage Competitor Matrix

### Platform Competitors

| Platform | Coverage | Refresh | Revenue Data | Key Limitation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Steam Spy | Steam only | 24h | Owners only, no revenue | Free, limited depth |
| Newzoo GPM | 4 platforms | Daily-monthly | Full revenue/retention | Enterprise pricing |
| Sensor Tower VGI | 3 platforms | 12h | UA linkage | No Steam |
| VG Insights | Steam only | Daily | +/-15% accuracy for 84% of games | Steam only |
| GameAnalytics | SDK-based | Real-time | Opt-in data only | Opt-in bias |

### Market Gaps PlaySage Can Exploit

1. No tool unifies Steam + Epic + Switch + PlayStation + Xbox in one index
2. Sub-hour refresh absent across all competitors (best is 12h from Sensor Tower)
3. Behavioural/psychographic segmentation is surface-level everywhere
4. Scenario modelling is manual across all platforms

### PlaySage Ten USPs

(1) Sub-hour refresh vs 12-24h competitors. (2) True cross-store index (5 stores). (3) AI scenario sandbox with causal ML. (4) Behavioural lens from NBI psychographic survey. (5) Live-ops alerts with NLP patch diff. (6) Transparent accuracy meter with margin of error per datapoint. (7) Indie-first pricing (free under USD 100k gross revenue). (8) Private data calibration via secure vault ingest. (9) API-native design. (10) Integrated NBI consulting escalation. [source: chatgpt_685b1bcc]
