## What This Bank Knows

- **Consolidation wave at record scale:** Q1 2026 gaming M&A hit $7.7B across 52 deals (3x Q1 2025). The $55B PIF-led EA take-private is the largest gaming LBO in history, pending CFIUS clearance by 30 June 2026. Take-Two is signalling acquisitions funded by GTA 6 revenue (launching 19 November 2026). Turkish mobile studios (Loom Games at $1B, Grand Games at $70M Series B) are producing outsized outcomes.

- **Platform economics restructured:** Google Play commission dropped from 30% to 20% following the Epic settlement -- the single largest change to mobile developer economics in a decade. Fortnite's global iOS return pulled 3.4M downloads in its first week, with MENA and EU demand far exceeding the US. Valve is preparing a multi-SKU Steam Machine hardware launch. Xbox Project Helix (2027-2028) introduces a unified GDK targeting console, PC, and cloud from a single build.

- **Live-service model in structural failure:** Sony has dismantled its PS5-era GaaS strategy across five sequential failures (Concord, Firewalk, Bluepoint, Destruction AllStars). Rec Room shut down ($3.5B peak valuation, never profitable). Survios VR studio effectively closed. The GDC 2026 survey found 28% of developers lost a job in two years, one in three US developers experienced a layoff.

- **Regulatory convergence on monetisation:** EU mandatory cancellation button live 19 June 2026. FTC secured $20M Genshin settlement with age-gating and odds disclosure requirements. UK ASA loot box advertising enforcement active 26 May 2026. India PROG Rules in force 1 May 2026. Brazil gaming tax incentives framework active. All major markets moving in the same direction simultaneously on transparency and age gating.

- **AI investment is stratospheric and uneven:** HoYoverse committed $14.7B over three years -- dwarfing all other single-company gaming AI commitments. Morgan Stanley (April 2026) projects AI could create $22B in annual profits by halving development costs. Unreal Engine 6 announced. Roblox challenging Unity/Unreal with AI-powered professional dev tools. Xbox, by contrast, wound down its Copilot gaming products under new CEO Asha Sharma.
