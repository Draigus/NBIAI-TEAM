# Bank Schema: personal_insights

## Purpose
Glen's accumulated strategic thinking, decision patterns, and business philosophy. The "why" behind NBI's direction — useful for ensuring consistency across sessions and informing AI-assisted work.

## Sections

### Executive Summary
3-5 sentences on the themes in Glen's decision-making.

### Strategic Decisions
Major decisions with reasoning. Format: what was decided, what alternatives were considered, why this path was chosen. Each entry traces to a specific session or conversation.

### Rejected Approaches
Approaches Glen explicitly decided against, and why. Prevents the system from re-suggesting rejected ideas.

### Business Philosophy
Recurring principles that guide NBI's work. Pricing philosophy, quality standards, client selection criteria, growth approach.

### Working Patterns
How Glen prefers to work. Tool preferences, communication style, review processes, what he delegates vs. keeps.

### Client Relationship Approaches
How Glen builds and maintains client relationships. Communication cadence, trust-building patterns, scope management.
