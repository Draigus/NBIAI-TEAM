# Bank Schema: industry_current

## Purpose
Current state of the gaming industry — deals, shifts, platform changes, regulatory developments. Time-sensitive intelligence with a 7-day shelf life for primary entries; entries older than 30 days get archived.

## Sections

### Executive Summary
3-5 sentences on the current market picture.

### Acquisitions & Funding
Recent deals: who acquired/invested in whom, how much, what for, what it signals. Table format preferred.

### Platform Changes
App Store rule updates, Steam algorithm changes, console certification shifts, new platform launches. Direct impact on NBI's clients.

### Market Data
Actual numbers: revenue figures, player counts, market sizes, growth rates. Only include data with cited sources.

### Technology Shifts
Engine changes, AI tools for game development, new middleware, tooling trends relevant to studios of 20-100 people.

### Regulatory
EU Digital Services Act, loot box regulation, age rating changes, data protection updates affecting games.

### Competitive Landscape
Studio closures, layoffs, new studio formations, team movements. Market shape changes.
