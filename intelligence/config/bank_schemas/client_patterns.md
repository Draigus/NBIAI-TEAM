# Bank Schema: client_patterns

## Purpose
Cross-client learnings from NBI engagements. What approaches work, what pricing lands, what proposals win, and what to avoid. Anonymised where necessary — patterns, not client-specific data.

## Sections

### Executive Summary
3-5 sentences on the engagement patterns NBI has observed.

### Engagement Approaches That Worked
What advisory structures, cadences, and formats produced the best client outcomes. Specific examples anonymised where needed.

### Pricing That Landed
Day rates, project fees, retainer structures that clients accepted. What price points work for what scopes. Context matters — a 55-person studio vs. a 10-person indie.

### Proposals That Won
Structural patterns in proposals that converted. What sections, what level of detail, what framing.

### Delivery Patterns
How NBI delivers work effectively. Sprint cadence for advisory, reporting frequency, communication channels.

### Common Client Challenges
Problems that appear across multiple clients. Production bottlenecks, scaling pains, fundraising gaps.

### What To Avoid
Approaches that didn't work, pricing that was rejected, engagement structures that created friction.
