# CH Simon/Robin/Glen Intro — Simon Background, Game Vision, AI Policy

## Key Content

Pre-start intro call with Simon Woodroffe (incoming Head of Design) and Robin. Simon's background: Simon the Sorcerer creator (started age 16); Epic Games designer (Kinect, Sea of Thieves, R&D); left Epic due to toxic culture on Disney collaboration. Epic-Disney context: not genuine partnership, transactional; Epic protected Fortnite, treated Disney project as side effort; high turnover; project designed as "front door to Disney" combining physical/digital. Roblox flagged as major Epic threat — 10x Fortnite engagement; users ageing up with Roblox rather than transitioning to Fortnite. Core principle now established studio-wide: build a great MMO first, platform features secondary; AMA reinforced this. Game references agreed: WoW for systems/feel, Guild Wars 2 for hitbox-based combat, ESO for live service model, Final Fantasy XIV for positive community. Design team composition (8–9 people): combat designer, technical designer, 2 juniors, senior designer, Gary (head level design), audio, writer, Yorgos, Valeria (product oversight). AI policy approach: currently used as augmentation across studio; Glen uses for research, competitive analysis, legal review; Simon experienced with AI prototyping and system visualisation; formal policy pending — approach will be "common sense" guidelines.
