# 1:1 Mustafa — Performance Confrontation, Burnout Admission, and Accountability Reset

## Key Content

Glen confronted Mustafa directly on pattern of underperformance since stepping down from CTO. Mustafa admitted immediately: confidence took massive hit, doing bare minimum, burnt out and doesn't know how to fix it. Full litany of incidents: performance reviews one-lined despite reminders (Glen meat-shielded him from leadership), told interview candidate Nia he was burnt out mid-interview (cost Glen an hour recovering candidate), took vacation without telling anyone (product council spent 30 minutes trying to locate him), shared confidential product council info with staff twice, submitted unvetted candidate for full stack role, "piece of cake" responses hiding disengagement. Glen named specific people being hurt: Hannah, Valeria, himself, the studio. Mustafa's root cause: isolation — "I don't have anyone to talk to... I've never had anyone to just sit down and talk about these things." Turning point when Glen said "we need you, we care" — Mustafa said that fixed a lot of the negative things. Full ownership: "I have been absolutely a passenger rather than leading." Resolution: weekly 1:1s booked, open door/Slack policy, Glen takes partial accountability for silence that enabled the drift. Glen shared Barb cautionary tale about studios that burn people out. Warning remains: visible behaviour change or departure, but framed through support not punishment.

## Decisions / Insights

- [Mustafa] admitted: doing bare minimum since CTO step-down, confidence destroyed, burnt out
- [Mustafa] identified root cause: isolation and lack of feedback — no one to talk to for months
- [Glen] decided: weekly 1:1s mandatory, open door policy for Mustafa
- [Glen] observed: his own silence enabled the drift — patience was wrong approach, should have confronted earlier
- [Glen] observed: when someone is isolated and burning out, expressing "we need you" can be more effective than listing failures
- [Mustafa] committed: "I will sort it out" — full ownership of passenger behaviour
- [Glen] warned: this is final warning — visible change or studio parts ways, but support offered first
- [Glen] insight: separate "burnt out and disengaged" (solvable with support) from "attention to detail and skill gaps" (different problem)
