# Pricing Analysis Project — Methodology & BD Pipeline

## Pricing Index Methodology (Detailed)
- Competitive pricing index for 10 sports games. Game selection via SensorTower/App Annie: Sports category, launched Apr 2024-2026, F2P, IAP revenue >60%.
- Screenshot capture workflow: iOS and Android, all tiers, OCR via Claude/Gemini in batches of 5.
- Tier standardisation: Starter (USD 0.99-4.99), Mid (USD 4.99-19.99), Premium (USD 19.99+), Mega (USD 49.99+).
- Regional pricing via VPN (NordVPN/ExpressVPN): US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, Canada.
- Historical pricing: SensorTower, SteamDB, Archive.org, Reddit/Discord, gaming news, earnings calls.
- Conversion rate estimation: ARPU calculation, IAP ARPU, sensitivity analysis with +/-50% average purchase amount.
