# PlayGOALS Project Plan 14 Apr -- Pricing Strategy, Monetisation, Live Ops

## Key Content

PlayGOALS: football game launching mid-May 2026, 400,000 players tested since 2022 through gradual horizontal development. Three large playtests (June, December, March) with positive feedback and strong retention. Cross-platform: PC (Steam, Epic), Xbox, PlayStation at launch; Nintendo Switch and mobile 2027; global release with Japanese/Chinese/Korean localisation. MONETISATION: player packs, kits, balls, celebrations. Non-licensed procedurally generated players with unique stats and RPG progression. Players retire at 30-34 creating natural sink. Skill vs purchase balance targeted 80/20 (purchased advantages help but do not override skill). Tiered player system: basic, common, epic. Legend system allows keeping retired favourites with tournament restrictions. PRICING: existing pricing matrix needs external validation. Premium currency through platform stores (6 price points $5.99-$19.99). Regional pricing across North America, Mexico, Europe. Platform review 9+ days, two-week deadline for submission. LIVE OPS: weekly content updates with monthly themes, seasonal events (World Cup drives higher monetisation), no server wipes, no battle pass initially. DATA: current three-persona model may be too narrow -- k-means clustering recommended for behavioural analysis. No A/B testing conducted yet (Julius has King background). Revenue recognition at point of currency spend. Staging environment available for team with weekend playtests. Design review needed with Frans (frans@playgoals.com). Contact: Julius via Discord.
