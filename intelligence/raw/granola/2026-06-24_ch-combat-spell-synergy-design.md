# Couch Heroes: Synergistic Combat and Rune Cosmology Decisions

## Key Content

Settled design decisions from a 2026-06-24 game direction alignment session (Glen, Vardis, Robin):

**Synergistic combat -- non-negotiable core intent:**
- The spell interaction system is already designed in the GDD: wet + lightning = critical hit, etc.
- Synergistic combat is not a "nice to have" -- it is a core design pillar. This framing must not be softened in production conversations.
- Robin's rune system is well-suited to pairing mechanics (runes can be combined for interaction effects).
- Risk: the combat lead (Nadir) defaults to pure combat thinking when social interaction angle is raised. Routing social combat framing through Robin and Simon (not directly to Nadir) is the agreed approach.

**Rune cosmology -- settled:**
- All runes exist simultaneously from the start (not gated behind discovery).
- Spell unlocks are staggered by island/biome as the player progresses.
- Tutorial: lightning + healing rune pair. Next island: fire rune introduced. Subsequent biomes add further rune access.

**Combat design milestone structure:**
- Combat progression needs defined stages from current state to V1 -- named milestones, not open-ended exploration.
- Simon (lead designer) must work within discrete chapters, not indefinitely exploratory design.
- Producers tasked with delivering combat stages, blockers, and timeline by mid-next-week.

## Decisions / Insights

- Glen and Vardis decided: synergistic combat is non-negotiable -- it will be designed as a core system, not deferred.
- Glen decided: keep Simon to async messaging for now; weekly calls only once design is sufficiently focused. Not in these sessions yet.
- Vardis to share the spell synergy Miro board and ClickUp status effects doc with Glen and Robin as the design source of truth.
