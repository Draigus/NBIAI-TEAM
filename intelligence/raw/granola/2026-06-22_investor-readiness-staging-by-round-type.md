# Investor Readiness Staging by Round Type

## Key Content
Framework for what visual fidelity and system completeness is required at each investment stage for a game studio raising capital:

**Early-round investors (family offices, non-gaming funds, warm intros):**
- Proxy kit + concept art booklet + roadmap is sufficient
- Non-gaming investors can engage with the vision narrative; they don't need a playable build
- A concept art booklet alone enables a credible demo at early industry events (Gamescom cited)
- CEO-level investor can sell this stage easily: "I can sell Eskimos in that situation"

**Industry conferences (e.g. DICE, September timeline):**
- Needs a representative build on a PC in a demo room
- Must show the game, not a concept: avatars with purpose, combat, trading, partner art
- Avatar-centric systems are the hook for IP partners and gaming funds
- Big gaming funds will ask "where's the fun?" — systems must be visible and interactive
- Side-by-side comparison of proxy vs. mid-poly bake is a narrative device showing progress

**Later rounds (large gaming funds, institutional):**
- Full system depth required: animator counts, market segmentation, financial model
- CFO and finance lead needed in the room
- Players per zone targets, engagement metrics, and monetisation model must be articulate

**Cross-round narrative device:**
- Show meaningful visual progress from round open to round close
- Side-by-side proxy vs. mid-poly bake as a proof-of-velocity narrative

## Decisions / Insights
- Glen and CEO aligned: early investors don't need the game; they need the vision and the team
- CEO observed: the bar for non-gaming early-round investors is lower than studios typically assume
- Glen decided: avatar-centric systems (customisation, trading) are the priority for DICE-level readiness
- Pattern recognised: large gaming funds specifically probe for fun — system-level completeness doesn't substitute for playability signals

Production meeting between Glen, the studio CEO, and Head of Production at a ~70-person MMO studio, 2026-06-22. Studio is ~2-3 months from investor readiness and calibrating which systems to prioritise for each audience.
