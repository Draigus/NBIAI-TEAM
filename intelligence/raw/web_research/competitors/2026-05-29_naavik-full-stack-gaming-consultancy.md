# Naavik: Full-Stack Gaming Consultancy with 300+ Clients

## Key Content

Naavik (founded 2019, Delaware) operates as a three-pillar gaming advisory firm serving 300+ companies including Ubisoft, Square Enix, Meta, Epic Games, and Activision Blizzard. Their service stack:

**Strategy Consulting:** Market research, corporate strategy, game and economy design, gamification, live ops strategy, AI strategy, product management, brand and performance marketing.

**M&A and Investment Advisory:** Buy-side and sell-side M&A, target identification, valuation assessment, transaction structuring, fractional CFO/CSO. Uses a fixed-fee model explicitly to "remove potential for conflict of interest" -- positions as strategic advisory, not investment bank.

**FractionalTalent:** 30+ named consultants across 8 categories (data science, engineering, game design, marketing, narrative, production, QA, visual/audio). 3-month trial, 6-month minimum engagement, "tailored pricing." Covers roles from CTO/CPO level down to specialist QA and analytics.

Team pedigrees cited: Zynga, EA, Disney, Gameloft, King, Rovio, Wooga. They claim to have worked with 250+ startups, game studios, and financial institutions.

## Decisions / Insights

Naavik is NBI's closest direct competitor in the gaming advisory space. Key differences:
- **Naavik is US-based and VC-ecosystem oriented.** NBI is UK-based with deeper European and MENA client relationships.
- **Naavik's fractional talent service is a significant differentiator** that NBI does not currently offer. This is a potential service gap or deliberate scope boundary.
- **Naavik's M&A fixed-fee model** is worth noting -- they explicitly avoid commission-based advisory, which signals a market preference against traditional banking fees.
- **Naavik does NOT appear to offer production consulting, studio operations, or HR/people advisory.** This is a gap NBI covers through its producer, head_of_people, and production_consultant roles.
- **No published pricing.** All engagement terms are custom/tailored.

## Context

Naavik has grown from a newsletter/podcast operation (2019) to a full consultancy. Their 2025 recap emphasised growth to 300+ companies served. They have strong content marketing (daily digest, podcast, deep dives) that acts as a lead generation engine -- a model NBI does not currently replicate.
