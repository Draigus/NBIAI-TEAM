# Boutique F2P and Game Economy Consultancies: Fragmented Specialist Market

## Key Content

Three specialist consultancies occupy the game economy / F2P niche alongside larger firms:

**F2P Games Consulting (gamesconsulting.net):** Solo practitioner (Nick Murray) offering game design, economy design, product management, live ops, monetisation strategy, due diligence, and workshops. Clients include Rovio, Nordisk Games, Goodgame Studios, Nitro Games, Cherrypick Games, and 10+ others. Testimonials praise "encyclopedic knowledge" and practical KPI improvement focus. Free initial consultation via Calendly.

**Game Economist Consulting (gameeconomistconsulting.com):** Solo practitioner (Phillip Black, @econosopher) offering economic design and counsel, live game economy assessments ("State-of-the-Union"), and analytics/product support. Three engagement models: Discovery Sprint (32 hours), One-Shot (fixed deliverables), and Retainer. Covers mobile/HD games, blockchain game economies, and non-gaming gamification (theme parks, social media). No named clients. Also runs "The Game Economist Project" connecting academic economists to game companies.

**Mobile Free to Play (mobilefreetoplay.com):** Game design consulting with F2P specialisation. Hire-a-consultant model. Details limited from search results.

## Decisions / Insights

The game economy consulting space is dominated by solo practitioners and micro-consultancies, not firms. This reveals several things:
1. **No one has scaled game economy consulting into a proper firm.** The market is fragmented across individual experts. This is either because the TAM is too small or because the expertise is too rare to hire.
2. **The solo practitioner model means limited capacity and no bench depth.** A client whose project overlaps with another engagement is out of luck.
3. **NBI's game_economy_consultant role gives it an advantage** -- having game economy expertise embedded within a broader advisory offering (with production, people, data, and strategy) is more valuable than an isolated economy specialist.
4. **Pricing is opaque across all three.** No published rates. The 32-hour "Discovery Sprint" from Game Economist Consulting is the most transparent engagement structure.
5. **Rovio appears as a client across multiple consultancies** (F2P Games Consulting, Naavik, and historically others) -- indicating that even large studios shop across consultants rather than committing to one firm.

## Context

These consultancies serve the mid-tier mobile F2P market -- studios with live games that need economy tuning, monetisation optimisation, or live ops strategy. They compete on individual reputation and network rather than firm brand.

## Applicability

NBI should position its game economy offering as part of a full-service advisory package, not as a standalone specialism. The solo practitioner competitors cannot offer the breadth NBI provides. When NBI encounters pure economy design work that does not require broader advisory, these firms could serve as referral partners or subcontractors.
