## Summary

Griffin Gaming Partners (Santa Monica-based VC firm focused exclusively on gaming) launched the $100M Special Opportunities Fund. Key differentiators:

- **Revenue-share model, not equity:** Funding provided in exchange for a share of game revenue, not studio equity. Deliberately eschewing traditional equity-based investment
- **Managing Director:** Tim Bender, CEO of publisher Hooded Horse
- **Transmedia focus:** Advisors include film/TV producer Dylan Clark and brand steward Russell Binder (Five Nights at Freddy's), signalling interest in games with cross-media IP potential
- **Already active:** 15 titles invested in, including 9 announced projects
- **Deal size:** Hundreds of thousands to low millions per deal
