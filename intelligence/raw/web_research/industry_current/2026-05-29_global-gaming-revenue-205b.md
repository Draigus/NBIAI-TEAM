## Summary

Key market data points for the gaming industry in 2026:

**Global market:**
- Total revenue projected at $205 billion in 2026, up 4.6% from $188.8 billion in 2025 (Newzoo)
- Mobile gaming: ~$107 billion (52% of total)
- Asia-Pacific leads at $87.6 billion (46% of global spend)

**US commercial gaming (Q1 2026):**
- Total revenue: $20.09 billion (+6.0% YoY)
- Traditional casino: $12.48 billion (+2.1%)
- iGaming: $3.04 billion (+20.7%)
- Sports betting: +8.9%
- 30 of 38 commercial gaming states saw revenue increases
- Executive sentiment at highest positive level since Q3 2022

**Softness signals:**
- Nexon guiding Q2 2026 as softest quarter, expecting -10% to +1% YoY revenue
- Nintendo Switch 2 production cuts (covered separately)
