## Summary

From June 19, 2026, EU regulations require all distance contracts (including game subscriptions, battle passes, and recurring IAPs) to include a visible cancellation button across apps and sites. Key points:

- Applies to all digital subscriptions sold to EU consumers
- Enforcement expected immediately via warning letters
- Part of broader 2026 regulatory tightening alongside Brazil's loot box ban for minors, Australia's 15+ rating for chance-based purchases, and Germany's push for 18+ gambling treatment of loot boxes
