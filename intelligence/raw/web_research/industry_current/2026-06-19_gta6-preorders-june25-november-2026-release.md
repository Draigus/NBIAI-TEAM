# GTA 6 Pre-Orders Confirmed June 25; November 19, 2026 Release Locked for PS5 and Xbox Series X/S

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Domain:** industry_current
**Category:** Market Data
**Relevance:** 7 | **Novelty:** 6 | **Actionability:** 6

## Summary

Rockstar Games has officially confirmed that Grand Theft Auto VI pre-orders open June 25, 2026, with cover art revealed featuring protagonists Jason and Lucia set in Vice City. The release date is locked at November 19, 2026 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S with no PC version at launch. Trailer 3 is widely anticipated to drop simultaneously on June 25 but has not been formally confirmed by Rockstar as of June 19. The pre-order opening is the first hard commercial signal for what is expected to be the largest entertainment launch in history by revenue.

## Key Facts

- GTA 6 pre-orders officially open June 25, 2026 (confirmed by Rockstar Games)
- Cover art confirmed: dual protagonists Jason and Lucia, Vice City setting
- Release date: November 19, 2026 for PS5 and Xbox Series X/S only
- No PC release date announced; PC version deferred (consistent with GTA 5 and Red Dead Redemption 2 precedent)
- Trailer 3 expected June 25 alongside pre-order launch; not formally confirmed as of June 19
- Mobile excluded from launch; no mobile release date
- Platforms at launch: PS5 and Xbox Series X/S only

## Strategic Significance

1. The November 2026 release locks in the single largest Q4 gaming event across console, compressing discretionary consumer spend and retailer attention for all other titles launching in that window. Any studio with a Q4 2026 date should treat October and November as severely elevated marketing cost and audience distraction periods.
2. Console-only launch with deferred PC is a deliberate sequencing play that will drive hardware sales and extend the commercial tail via PC launch 12-18 months later. This pattern (GTA 5, RDR2) is now an established Take-Two commercial strategy and a reference model for premium IP launch sequencing.
3. Pre-order volume data from June 25 onwards will be the first quantified signal of GTA 6 commercial scale, informing analyst forecasts, platform holder positioning, and publisher portfolio decisions through to the 2027 PC window.

## NBI Advisory Relevance

- Any NBI client planning a Q4 2026 or early 2027 launch should factor GTA 6's market saturation into timing decisions. This is not an avoidable risk -- it is a planning input.
- The console-only PC-deferred strategy is a current reference case for premium IP launch sequencing that clients in similar tiers may be evaluating as they consider their own platform strategy.
- Pre-order numbers, once public, will be the most current large-scale consumer demand signal available for NBI's forecasting and market-sizing work.
