## Summary

Subnautica 2 launched into Early Access on May 14, 2026 and sold over 2 million copies in its first 24 hours, confirming the $250M earnout payment from Krafton to Unknown Worlds founders. The backstory is extraordinary:

- Krafton acquired Unknown Worlds in 2021 for $500M upfront + up to $250M in contingent earnout payments
- In July 2025, Krafton fired founders Charlie Cleveland and Max McGuire plus CEO Ted Gill, allegedly to avoid triggering the earnout
- Krafton's CEO used a ChatGPT chatbot to plan the "takeover" strategy
- Krafton locked Unknown Worlds out of its own publishing platform and delayed the game's release
- A Delaware Chancery Court judge ordered Gill reinstated, returned control of the EA release schedule, and extended the earnout deadline to September 15, 2026
- The game's massive commercial success has now rendered the earnout dispute moot -- Krafton will pay
