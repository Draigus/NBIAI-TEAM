## Summary

Fortnite returned to Apple's App Store globally on May 19, 2026 (except Australia) and pulled 3.4 million iOS downloads in its first week. Detailed metrics:

- Daily installs surged from 19,000 (May 18) to 290,000 (May 19) -- a 1,408% increase
- Peak day: 674,000 installs on May 23
- **Regional breakdown:** Saudi Arabia led with 474,000, France 366,000, UK 307,000, US 151,000
- Epic declared the return as the "beginning of the end of the Apple tax"

The regional data is striking -- MENA and European markets significantly outpaced the US, suggesting pent-up mobile demand was highest in regions with fewer alternative gaming platforms.
