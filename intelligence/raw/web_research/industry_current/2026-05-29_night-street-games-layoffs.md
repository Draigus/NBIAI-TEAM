# Night Street Games Lays Off Staff After Last Flag Underperforms

**Domain:** industry_current

**Source:** Game Developer
**URLs:**

## Summary

Night Street Games, developer of Last Flag, has laid off approximately a dozen staff. The studio stated that the game's player count was not sufficient to "support additional development" beyond upcoming planned patches. The game will receive its committed patch support but no further content investment.

This sits within the broader 2026 layoff context: the GDC 2026 State of the Game Industry report found 28% of surveyed developers lost a job in the past two years, with one in three US developers experiencing a layoff. May 2026 also saw redundancies at Metacore, 2K, and MercurySteam, plus the near-total shutdown of Survios.
