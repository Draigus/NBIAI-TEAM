# HoYoverse Investing Up to $14.7B in AI Over Three Years

## Summary

HoYoverse co-founder and CEO Liu Wei announced during a May 15, 2026 presentation to postgraduate students in Beijing that the company will invest up to 100 billion yuan ($14.7 billion) in in-house AI development over three years. The investment covers:

- Application architecture and training systems
- GPU cluster infrastructure
- NPC enhancement systems for games
- Content generation capabilities
- Specific application to Petit Planet (upcoming life simulator for mobile and PC)

This dwarfs other gaming AI investments and positions HoYoverse (Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail) as potentially the largest single-company AI spender in the games industry.
