## Summary

Unity appointed Bernard Kim as an independent board director effective May 1, 2026. Kim's background:

- President of Publishing at Zynga (oversaw global marketing and user acquisition)
- Nearly a decade at Electronic Arts as SVP of Mobile Publishing
- CEO and board member of Match Group

The appointment coincides with board departures: Unity co-founder David Helgason and Avi Bar-Zeev are exiting. This signals Unity's continued pivot toward mobile gaming and monetisation expertise at the governance level, following the turbulent period after the runtime fee controversy.
