## Summary

New Xbox CEO Asha Sharma (replacing Phil Spencer, who retired in February 2026) has executed a sweeping leadership overhaul. The division reverts from "Microsoft Gaming" to the "Xbox" brand. Key changes:

- **New hires from tech:** Jared Palmer (ex-GitHub/Vercel, now Product/Engineering/Dev Tools), Tim Allen (ex-Instacart, Head of Design), Jonathan McKay (ex-OpenAI ChatGPT growth, Head of Growth), David Schloss (Subscription/Cloud Business)
- **Departures:** Kevin Gammill (VP User Experience/Game Dev, leaving), Roanne Sones (VP Devices/Ecosystem, to advisor role)
- **AI pivot:** Xbox is winding down Copilot on mobile and halting Copilot on console development
- **Strategy:** Prioritising core console/PC experiences, scrapping certain AI projects, re-evaluating game exclusivity, promising biweekly console updates through year-end

## NBI Relevance

Major platform strategy shift. The hiring of tech product leaders from outside gaming (OpenAI, Vercel, Instacart) signals Xbox is treating gaming as a tech product problem rather than a content problem. The Copilot wind-down contradicts the industry AI narrative. NBI advisory clients building on Xbox ecosystem need to understand the new leadership's priorities.
