## Summary

Rec Room, the VR/cross-platform social gaming platform once valued at $3.5 billion, will permanently shut down on June 1, 2026 at noon Pacific. After a decade and 150 million registered players, the company never achieved sustainable profitability. Key factors:

- VR market shift and broader gaming headwinds made the path to profitability untenable
- Token earnings stopped May 18; RR+ memberships extended through shutdown
- Refund requests must be filed by June 15 via Rec Room website
- Room/invention data can be downloaded but not in a working format (requires rebuilding in Unity)

The $3.5B valuation (reached during the 2021 VR hype cycle) makes this one of the largest value destructions in the UGC/social gaming space.
