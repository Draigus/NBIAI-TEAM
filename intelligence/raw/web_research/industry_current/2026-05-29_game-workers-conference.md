## Summary

The Game Workers Conference (GWC), a new union-led industry event, held its inaugural edition on May 22-23, 2026. Key details:

- **Format:** Free online sessions with optional in-person watch parties in LA, Montreal, and New York
- **Organiser:** United Videogame Workers of CWA (UVW-CWA) plus allied unions and worker organisations
- **Positioning:** "By workers, for workers" -- explicitly an alternative to GDC and other publisher-led events
- **Content:** Industry skills, project postmortems, union campaigns, movement-building sessions
- **Participants:** Game Awards Future Class members, gaming journalists

This follows broader unionisation momentum in the industry, with UVW-CWA established as an industry-wide union advocating for worker dignity. Combined with the GDC State of Industry report finding 28% of developers lost a job in the past two years, labour organising is accelerating.
