# Nintendo Raises Switch 2 Prices Globally, Cuts Production

## Summary

Nintendo is implementing significant price increases for Switch 2 and legacy hardware across all major markets, while simultaneously cutting production:

**Price increases:**
- Japan (effective May 25): Switch 2 from 49,980 to 59,980 yen (+20%), Switch OLED +26%, Switch +33%, Switch Lite +36%
- US (effective September 1): Switch 2 from $449.99 to $499.99 (+11%)
- Canada (September 1): +$50 CAD (+8%)
- Europe (September 1): +30 euros (+6%)
- Nintendo Switch Online subscriptions also increasing in Japan and South Korea

**Production cut:**
- Q2 2026 output reduced from 6M to 4M units (-33%) due to weak US holiday sales
- Despite this, lifetime sales exceed 17M units since June 2025 launch
- Japan demand remains strong; weakness concentrated in US and other Western markets

**Stated reason:** "Various changes in market conditions" -- Nintendo's president previously cited AI data centre demand creating memory component shortages that "put pressure on profitability"
