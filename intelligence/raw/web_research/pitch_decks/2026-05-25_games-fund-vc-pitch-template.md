# The Games Fund: VC-Published Pitch Deck Template and Resource Kit

## Key Content (max 200 words)

The Games Fund (ranked #1 early-stage gaming investor in 2024, #4 across all stages) publishes a free resource kit for game studio founders. Centrepiece is a Google Slides pitch deck template (publicly accessible) described as containing "all the information investors need to see" with the caveat that format is optional. Complementary resources: (1) P&L example spreadsheet for due diligence, (2) Company budget calculator for estimating team size, dev costs, hiring plans, and fundraising requirements. The Fund also curates recommended reading: Venture Deals (Brad Feld -- VC mechanics), Hitmakers (audience engagement), Creativity Inc. (Pixar's creative process). Podcast recommendations: Elite Game Developers, Naavik's Metacast, Deconstructor of Fun. Data tools: AppMagic (competitive intelligence), InvestGame (deal tracking), GamesBeat (industry news). The Fund invests globally, positions itself as a "bridge between international companies and local developers," and focuses on early-stage gaming companies. Their investment thesis prioritises helping studios integrate into the global gaming ecosystem.
