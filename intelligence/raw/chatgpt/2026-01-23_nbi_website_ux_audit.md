# NBI Website UX Audit Methodology

## Key Content
Ten-dimension scoring rubric for B2B consulting website assessment: (1) First-5-seconds clarity (who, what, for whom, outcome). (2) Message hierarchy and information scent. (3) Visual hierarchy (typography, spacing, contrast). (4) Brand credibility (enterprise polish, consistency). (5) Trust signals (case studies, certifications, client logos, testimonials). (6) Calls-to-action (clarity, placement, friction). (7) Content quality (specificity, differentiation). (8) Accessibility basics (WCAG 2.2 AA). (9) Mobile experience. (10) Conversion readiness (contact flow, forms, objections handled). Each scored 1-10 with evidence. Must answer: what does the company appear to do based only on hero and first scroll? Top 3 points of confusion? Strongest part? Every critique must include page URL, location, issue, impact, and recommendation with concrete before/after copy.
