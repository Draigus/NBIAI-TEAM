# Two-Lane AI Adoption Programme for US Government-Adjacent Consulting

## Key Content
End-to-end project plan for integrating AI across regulated and non-regulated work at a US consulting firm. Two-lane model: Lane A (CUI/regulated) uses approved-only tools with US-only data residency; Lane B (non-CUI research) allows broader tooling after vetting. Controlled merge procedure moves material from Lane B to Lane A via sanitisation checklist, human reviewer sign-off, citation validation with Source Map, and Claim Ledger. Work Classification Guide with decision tree (default: route to Lane A until classified). Tool vetting framework: security questionnaire, contract language review, foreign provenance checks (no Chinese or foreign-based toolsets), project-specific government constraints. Phase 0 discovery required before any deployment. Pilot structured with hypothesis testing rather than open-ended adoption.
