# SoW Finalisation Report Structure for Game Studio Consulting

## Key Content
15-section C-level-consumable report structure for finalising a consulting SoW: (1) Executive Summary with top 8 risks. (2) Background and Intent. (3) Scope of Work with in/out boundaries anchored to CSA clause references. (4) Deliverables and Acceptance with measurable acceptance tests and evidence requirements. (5) Delivery Plan and Milestones with entry/exit criteria. (6) Technical Architecture Alignment. (7) Cross-Play Readiness. (8) Design and Product Readiness. (9) KPI Tree and Measurement with target bands and owners. (10) Risk Register top 20 ranked by impact x likelihood. (11) Staffing Plan and RACI. (12) Commercials and Change Control. (13) Governance and Communication. (14) Assumptions, Dependencies, and Open Questions. (15) Appendices with Evidence Table (claim, source, date, confidence, gap). Glen's meta-prompt also specifies multi-role red teaming: Production finds two issues, Engineering finds two, and the report documents how each was fixed.
