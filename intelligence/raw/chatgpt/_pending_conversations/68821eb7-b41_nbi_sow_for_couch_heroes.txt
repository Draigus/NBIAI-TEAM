### 1. Overview  
This Statement of Work ("SOW") is issued under and subject to the terms and conditions of the Consulting Services Agreement (“CSA”) between Couch Heroes Ltd. (“Client”) and NBI Consulting Ltd. (“Consultant”). This SOW sets forth the scope, objectives, deliverables, responsibilities, and terms applicable to the Consultant’s services relating to MVP delivery readiness and production operations improvement.

---

### 2. Objectives  
The primary objectives of this engagement are:

- To ensure successful delivery of the MVP milestone in October 2025.
- To identify and optimize production methodology across departments.
- To assess technical alignment between the Game Design Document (GDD), core design pillars, and current implementation.
- To diagnose and address cultural and behavioral barriers within mid-tier leadership.
- To improve internal communications and operational transparency.
- To assess alignment of staff roles, skills, and responsibilities.
- To identify and implement process improvements across game development, business operations, and people management functions.

---

### 3. Scope of Work  

The Consultant will undertake the following activities:

#### 3.1. MVP Delivery Planning & Milestone Confidence  
- Conduct structured sprint review and feature readiness audit.  
- Build a roadmap to MVP delivery with risk flags and dependency locks.  
- Create a milestone assurance framework with clear ownership and cross-functional validation.  

#### 3.2. Production Methodology Review  
- Audit current production framework (agile, hybrid, or bespoke) used across game and content teams.  
- Benchmark studio’s methodology against best practices from peer and aspirational studios.  
- Deliver a revised production approach optimized for phase, team size, and culture fit.  

#### 3.3. Technical Assessment  
- Deep-dive analysis of GDD against implemented feature sets and systems.  
- Assess functional fit of technical implementations with core game design vision.  
- Identify gaps, risks, or scope creep in codebase relative to the intended player experience.

#### 3.4. Leadership Engagement & Cultural Friction  
- Diagnose behavioral friction below C-level leadership through interviews and diagnostics.  
- Identify root causes for unwillingness to collaborate or escalate risk proactively.  
- Recommend interventions to reset team dynamics and accountability practices.

#### 3.5. Communication Dynamics  
- Map current communication pathways and pain points.  
- Identify bottlenecks, tool misuse, or over-reliance on siloed updates.  
- Propose new cadences, touchpoints, and escalation protocols.

#### 3.6. Role Alignment & Talent Fit  
- Evaluate job-role clarity and cross-functional expectations.  
- Map skills inventory to project needs and phase-appropriate staffing.  
- Highlight misalignments, underutilization, or potential upskilling opportunities.

#### 3.7. Process Optimization  
- Conduct an audit across game dev, business, and people operations.  
- Identify inefficient workflows, redundant checkpoints, or decision-making gaps.  
- Recommend structured changes to improve speed, autonomy, and outcomes.

---

### 4. Deliverables

---

### 1. Purpose  
This Statement of Work (“SOW”) is issued under the Consulting Services Agreement (“CSA”) between Couch Heroes Ltd. (“Client”) and NBI Consulting Ltd. (“Consultant”) and outlines the services to be provided in relation to production effectiveness, MVP milestone assurance, technical coherence, leadership behavior, and organizational optimization.

---

### 2. Objectives  

The engagement is intended to support Couch Heroes in achieving the following objectives:

- Enable a high-confidence delivery of the October MVP milestone.  
- Diagnose and improve the current production methodology to suit organizational scale and project phase.  
- Validate technical alignment between GDD, core gameplay design, and live implementation.  
- Address behavioral and cultural resistance among mid-level leadership.  
- Resolve internal communication breakdowns across teams.  
- Assess talent-role alignment and address skill gaps or misplacement.  
- Recommend improvements across game development, operational, and people processes.

---

### 3. Scope of Services  

#### 3.1 MVP Delivery Alignment  
- Conduct MVP readiness workshops with product and engineering leads.  
- Audit current backlog, feature readiness, risk posture, and dependency blocks.  
- Establish clear ownership of remaining deliverables and cross-functional touchpoints.

**Deliverable:** MVP Risk & Milestone Readiness Report  
**Due:** Five (5) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.2 Production Methodology Assessment  
- Review current sprint cadence, ritual efficacy, and tooling stack.  
- Evaluate process maturity against team size, complexity, and game phase.  
- Recommend streamlined, adaptive methodology fit for October scope.

**Deliverable:** Production Methodology Audit & Recommendations  
**Due:** Ten (10) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.3 Technical Fit-for-Purpose Review  
- Cross-reference GDD features with codebase implementation and in-engine builds.  
- Identify divergences from intended core gameplay, potential refactors, and fidelity loss.  
- Provide structured remediation options and impact rating.

**Deliverable:** Technical Alignment & Gap Assessment  
**Due:** Twelve (12) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.4 Leadership Behavior & Accountability Dynamics  
- Facilitate diagnostic sessions and anonymized feedback loops with non-C-level leadership.  
- Surface resistance points, passivity patterns, and decision paralysis origins.  
- Recommend behavioral interventions, rituals, or delegation redesigns.

**Deliverable:** Cultural Friction & Leadership Dynamics Brief  
**Due:** Fifteen (15) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.5 Communication Systems and Cadence  
- Map current intra-team and cross-discipline communication practices.  
- Audit reliance on tools vs. meetings; identify signal-loss or siloing patterns.  
- Recommend cadence changes, visibility layers, and trust-building interventions.

**Deliverable:** Studio Communications Flow Report  
**Due:** Seventeen (17) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.6 Talent Fit & Role Alignment  
- Inventory staff roles, documented responsibilities, and actual activities.  
- Map skillsets against project-critical needs for next two quarters.  
- Highlight misalignment, under-leverage, or overextension of individuals.

**Deliverable:** Role Alignment and Skill Fit Matrix  
**Due:** Nineteen (19) business days after project initiation  

---

#### 3.7 Process Optimization – Game, Business, and People Ops  
- Conduct structured audits across dev workflow, studio ops, and people processes.  
- Identify friction points, handoff delays, and duplicated decision chains.  
- Recommend phased process upgrades with appropriate accountability structures.

**Deliverable:** Cross-Domain Process Optimization Report  
**Due:** Twenty-one (21) business days after project initiation  

---

### 4. Client Responsibilities  
- Timely access to personnel and materials relevant to each workstream.  
- Dedicated point of contact for coordination and approvals.  
- Reasonable availability of team leads for interviews, workshops, or diagnostics.

---

### 5. Consultant Responsibilities  
- Deliver all workstreams in accordance with the timing and structure set forth herein.  
- Maintain confidentiality and professional discretion per CSA.  
- Escalate potential blockers or scope changes promptly.

---

### 6. Fees and Invoicing  
Fees shall be invoiced according to the CSA terms. Any additional implementation work beyond this SOW will require mutual written agreement through a Change Order.

---

### 7. Term & Termination  
This SOW shall remain in effect until all deliverables are completed or unless terminated earlier in accordance with the CSA. Standard terms for termination, liability, and dispute resolution apply.
