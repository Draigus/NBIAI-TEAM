Can you take apxiban and mounjaro at the same time


[ASSISTANT]
There are no known direct drug interactions between apixaban (a blood thinner) and Mounjaro (tirzepatide, a medication for type 2 diabetes). However, both medications can have side effects, and taking them together could potentially increase the risk of side effects such as bleeding (from apixaban) or gastrointestinal issues (from Mounjaro).

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before starting these medications together, as they can evaluate your specific health condition, monitor potential interactions, and adjust dosages if necessary.

Find me a recipe for dried sausage from the Hillcountry in Central Texas

[ASSISTANT]
Here are two traditional dried sausage recipes from the Hill Country in Central Texas:

**Recipe 1: Texas Style Link Sausage**  
This recipe uses a blend of 8 pounds of ground lean beef, pork, or venison, with 2 pounds of pork or beef fat. Spices include kosher salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, mustard seed, and optional additions like cheese or jalapenos. The sausage is stuffed into hog casings, smoked for at least 3 hours until the internal temperature reaches 165°F, and then dried for 24 hours for a snack-like consistency. For a complete guide, visit [Wild Ed's Texas Outdoors](http://wildedtx.blogspot.com/2012/10/texas-style-link-sausage.html)【14†source】.

**Recipe 2: Texas Hill Country Sausage**  
This version combines 4 pounds of pork butt with fat and 2 pounds of beef chuck or round with fat, along with onion, garlic, fresh sage, salt, black pepper, crushed red pepper, and cayenne. The mixture is stuffed into hog casings, then smoked at 225°F for two hours with oak or mesquite. You can find full details at [Foodgeeks](https://foodgeeks.com/recipes/texas-hill-country-sausage-16587.html)【15†source】. 

Both recipes provide an authentic taste of Central Texas and allow for personalization with different spices or seasonings.
