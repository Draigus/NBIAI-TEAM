## Pre-Prompt for Cost & Profit Analysis of T&M Contract (4 Staff)

**Objective:**  
Derive the total contract cost, margin, and profit for a time and materials (T&M) billed engagement involving 4 staff members. Ensure that all direct and indirect cost drivers are included, and provide both per-staff and aggregate financial outputs.  

---

### 1. Contract Scope Inputs
- **Duration of engagement:** (weeks, months, or total hours)  
- **Expected utilization rate per staff member:** (% billable hours per week)  
- **Client billing rates:** (hourly/daily rate per staff role; list separately if different roles)  
- **Overtime or weekend premium rates, if applicable**  

---

### 2. Staff & Labor Costs
For each of the 4 staff:
- **Base salary / day-rate equivalent**  
- **Fully weighted labor cost per hour/day** (including employer contributions: payroll tax, pension, NI, insurance)  
- **Expected billable hours per week**  
- **Effective cost per billable hour**  

---

### 3. Overheads & Indirect Costs
- **Proportional overhead allocation per staff** (office, equipment, software licenses, HR, IT, finance, admin, etc.)  
- **Travel & expenses** (onsite visits, conferences, client workshops)  
- **Facilities costs** (if dedicated to this project)  
- **Training / onboarding time** (non-billable but still a cost)  

---

### 4. Revenue Calculations
- **Total billable hours per staff over contract duration**  
- **Revenue per staff member = Billable hours × Billing rate**  
- **Total revenue across all 4 staff**  

---

### 5. Cost Calculations
- **Direct labor cost = Staff fully weighted cost × hours worked**  
- **Overhead allocation = % of overhead applied per staff member**  
- **Total project cost = Sum of labor + overhead + expenses**  

---

### 6. Profitability Metrics
- **Gross Margin % = (Revenue – Direct Labor) ÷ Revenue**  
- **Contribution Margin % = (Revenue – Direct Labor – Allocated Overheads) ÷ Revenue**  
- **Absolute Profit = Revenue – Total Project Cost**  
- **Break-even billing rate per staff role** (minimum rate required to cover all costs)  

---

### 7. Scenario / Sensitivity Testing
- **Low case:** 70% utilization  
- **Expected case:** 85% utilization  
- **High case:** 95% utilization  
- **Impact of rate changes:** ±10% billing rate variation  
- **Impact of duration shifts:** +/– 1 month  

---

### 8. Reporting Outputs
Generate a **table** with the following columns for each staff member and totals:
- Staff Role  
- Billable Hours  
- Billing Rate  
- Revenue  
- Fully Weighted Cost  
- Overheads/Expenses  
- Total Cost  
- Gross Margin %  
- Profit  

Also generate **summary metrics**:
- Total Revenue  
- Total Costs (Labor + Overheads + Expenses)  
- Total Profit (absolute)  
- Profit Margin (%)
