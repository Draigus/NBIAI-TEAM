### **Playsage Human Capital & Organizational Health Services**

**1. Workforce Analytics Dashboards**  
- Headcount by function, seniority, and geography.  
- Attrition, retention, and tenure curves (D30, D90, 1Y, 2Y).  
- DEI mix and representation by role level.  
- Cost per employee vs. output (revenue proxy, feature velocity).  

**2. Organizational Diagnostics**  
- Cross-team collaboration index (using project/tool data).  
- Bottleneck mapping: where communication slows or fails.  
- Engagement heatmaps (pulse survey data + sentiment trends).  
- Skill gap analysis aligned to strategic goals.  

**3. Sentiment & Engagement Monitoring**  
- Real-time analysis of employee surveys, Slack/Teams chatter (optional, anonymized).  
- Topic modeling of verbatim feedback to surface “what’s changed.”  
- Correlation of engagement sentiment with productivity or retention dips.  

**4. Forecasting & Scenario Planning**  
- Attrition forecast (90-day, 1-year).  
- Hiring demand simulation: model the effect of growth plans on skill gaps.  
- Burnout & capacity models tied to project timelines and release cadences.  
- Scenario builder: “What if we scale X team by 20%?”  

**5. Market Benchmarking (Talent Intelligence)**  
- Salary benchmarks by role, region, and competitor cluster.  
- Comparative org charts for peer studios.  
- Hiring velocity of competitors (based on job postings and LinkedIn).  
- Forecasted wage inflation by skillset/market.  

**6. Alerts & Thresholds**  
- Automated notifications when attrition crosses X%.  
- Alerts for underrepresented roles or missing skills for planned launches.  
- Burnout risk alerts tied to work cadence and overtime data.  

**7. Recommendations Engine (“Org Sage”)**  
- Best-practice playbooks: “Intervene here” guidance.  
- Suggested interventions: e.g., run engagement pulse, rebalance project assignments, adjust comp bands.  
- Forecasted lift or risk reduction per action.  

**8. Integration Layer**  
- Connectors for HRIS (Workday, BambooHR), ATS, Slack/Teams.  
- Secure API for exporting workforce data to NBI consulting dashboards.  
- Client-facing sandbox with anonymized org patterns.  

**9. Organizational Health Scorecard**  
- Composite index blending retention, engagement, diversity, productivity, and cost.  
- Benchmark against industry medians and high-performing studios.  
- Track improvement over time with intervention tags.  

**10. Executive Reporting & Exports**  
- One-click PDF/PowerPoint of workforce health dashboards.  
- Customizable KPIs for board or investor reporting.  
- Historical comparisons and forward-looking forecasts included.
