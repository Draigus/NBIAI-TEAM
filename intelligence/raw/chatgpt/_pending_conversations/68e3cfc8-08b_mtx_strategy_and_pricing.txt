# Request for Work: MTX Strategy and Pricing Framework for Play Goals

## Objective
Provide Play Goals and Jonas with a decision-grade strategy for a pure-store monetisation model for a competitive football game on Xbox, PlayStation, and Steam, releasing before the World Cup, with later mobile. Focus on pricing, first-time purchase optimisation, platform-specific price ladders, elasticity tuning, sentiment risk for attribute-offset cosmetics, and a practical operating framework the team can run post-engagement.

## Background (from client brief)
- Platform plan: Xbox, PlayStation, Steam at launch, mobile later. Game Pass participation on console.  
- Store model: No Battle Pass. Pure store. First-party hard currency packs and an in-game store.  
- Catalogue: Skins and cosmetics. Skins include plus-minus attribute offsets.  
- Goals: Nail first purchase, set correct price points per platform, index against market comparators, and enable post-launch tuning for price elasticity.  
- Constraint: Team is new to console MTX. Desire to avoid pay-to-win while using attribute offsets.

## Scope of Work
1. **Discovery and Alignment**
   - Product, audience, and mode analysis. Content plan and Live Ops cadence expectations.  
   - Audit of SKU plan, rarity, bundles, and release calendar.

2. **Market and Price Indexing**
   - Competitive and adjacent benchmarking by platform and region for hard currency packs, cosmetics, bundles, and value density.  
   - Build price index ranges and platform-specific ladders tied to item archetypes.

3. **Store Architecture and Currency Design**
   - Hard currency SKU design for Xbox, PlayStation, and Steam.  
   - In-game store layout, value ladders, anchor SKUs, first purchase offers, attach tactics.

4. **Attribute-Offset Policy and Balance Guardrails**
   - Define allowed attribute variance for cosmetics.  
   - Create fairness boundaries, disclosure rules, and audit checks to avoid pay-to-win perception.

5. **Elasticity and Experimentation Framework**
   - Price tests, offer sequencing, limited-time events, and A/B governance.  
   - Elasticity estimation method and roll-out criteria per platform.

6. **Sentiment and Risk Management**
   - Sentiment polarisation map for attribute-offset items.  
   - Crisis triggers, messaging templates, and rollback plan.

7. **KPI and Telemetry Specification**
   - First-time purchase funnel metrics, attach rates, price sensitivity markers.  
   - Dashboard definitions and weekly operating rhythm.

8. **Delivery Kit**
   - Price ladders and targets by platform, item type, and bundle.  
   - Store playbook, test plan, governance checklist, and go-to-market recommendations for the pre-World Cup window.
