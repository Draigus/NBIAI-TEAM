### **User Research Analyst – Games Industry**  
**Location:** [Specify Location / Remote]  
**Type:** Full-time / Contract  

#### **About Us**  
NBI Games Consultancy partners with game studios to optimize development, launch, and live-service operations. We leverage cutting-edge research, behavioral science, and data analytics to help studios understand their players, enhance user experiences, and drive commercial success. Our team includes experts from top gaming companies like Blizzard, Ubisoft, Xbox, and EA, ensuring deep industry knowledge and actionable insights.  

#### **Role Overview**  
As a **User Research Analyst**, you will play a critical role in shaping the gaming experience by analyzing player behaviors, conducting user experience (UX) research, and delivering insights that drive design and development decisions. Your expertise will help studios refine game mechanics, improve accessibility, and maximize player retention through data-driven recommendations.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**  
#### **User Research & Playtesting**  
- Plan and execute **user experience (UX) research** to evaluate **game usability, balance, and player engagement**.  
- Design **playtest objectives and methodologies**, including surveys, interviews, focus groups, and telemetry analysis.  
- Recruit diverse participants that match target demographics, ensuring relevant player feedback.  
- Conduct **moderated and unmoderated playtesting**, capturing qualitative and quantitative insights.  
- Analyze **gameplay data, user interactions, and behavioral patterns** to identify friction points, engagement drivers, and opportunities for improvement.  
- Synthesize research findings into **actionable recommendations** for game designers, product managers, and executive teams.  

#### **Data Analysis & Competitive Research**  
- Perform **competitive analysis** by benchmarking key features, player behaviors, and monetization strategies across similar titles.  
- Collaborate with data analysts to integrate **game telemetry, heatmaps, and player progression data** into research findings.  
- Assist in developing **predictive models** to assess player retention, engagement risks, and monetization strategies.  
- Generate automated reports and dashboards for ongoing tracking of **key performance indicators (KPIs)**.  
- Contribute to **addressable market analysis** by evaluating platform, genre, and audience segmentation data.  

#### **Stakeholder Collaboration & Reporting**  
- Work closely with **game designers, UX teams, data scientists, and live operations teams** to align research with development goals.  
- Develop **comprehensive research reports, visual dashboards, and presentations** for internal and external stakeholders.  
- Translate complex research insights into **clear, actionable recommendations** for product improvement.  
- Present findings to **studio leadership, investors, and publishing partners** as needed.  

---

### **Qualifications & Skills**  

#### **Required:**  
- 3+ years of experience in **user research, UX testing, or data analytics**, preferably in gaming or interactive entertainment.  
- Strong proficiency in **qualitative and quantitative research methodologies**, including **surveys, A/B testing, usability studies, and telemetry analysis**.  
- Experience with **playtesting protocols**, behavioral research, and **cognitive heuristics analysis** in player interactions.  
- Familiarity with **game design principles, progression systems, and player motivation frameworks**.  
- Proficiency in **data analysis tools (Excel, SQL, Python, R) and survey platforms (Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, etc.)**.  
- Ability to synthesize complex data into **clear, compelling narratives** for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.  
- Passion for video games, with knowledge of **current industry trends, player behaviors, and emerging game technologies**.  

#### **Preferred:**  
- Experience working with **AAA or live-service games** in a research or analytics role.  
- Background in **psychology, human-computer interaction (HCI), cognitive science, or data science**.  
- Familiarity with **game telemetry systems, machine learning models for player behavior, or business intelligence tools**.  
- Experience conducting **cross-cultural research and international market analysis** in gaming.  

---

### **Why Join Us?**  
- **Work with top-tier game studios**: Our research influences major game franchises and innovative indie titles.  
- **Leverage cutting-edge behavioral science** to drive better game experiences.  
- **Collaborate with industry veterans** who have worked on some of the world’s most successful games.  
- **Flexible work environment**: Remote-friendly, adaptable to your location and schedule.  
- **Career growth and impact**: Play a pivotal role in shaping the future of gaming.  

If you're passionate about **player research, data-driven design, and making games better**, we'd love to hear from you.
