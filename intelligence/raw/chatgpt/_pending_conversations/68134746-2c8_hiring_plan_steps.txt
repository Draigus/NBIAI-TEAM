Here is a **detailed step-by-step hiring plan** you can directly add into **Microsoft Planner** as tasks (with buckets, checklists, and due dates). It's structured for project-based execution with clear milestones:

---

### **Bucket 1: Role Definition & Planning**
**1. Define Hiring Need**
- Why is this role required?
- Permanent or contract?
- Headcount budget confirmed?

**2. Draft Job Description**
- Responsibilities
- Required and preferred qualifications
- Team fit and reporting line

**3. Get Role Approval**
- Secure internal sign-off from HR, Finance, and leadership
- Confirm budget and compensation band

**4. Define Hiring Team & Roles**
- Recruiter
- Hiring Manager
- Interview panel members

**5. Set Timeline & KPIs**
- Target hire date
- Funnel metrics (e.g. # of interviews, pass rate)
- Time-to-fill and quality-of-hire goals

---

### **Bucket 2: Sourcing & Promotion**
**6. Create Job Posting**
- Final JD + internal branding
- Role benefits, EVP, and DEI language

**7. Post to Channels**
- Careers page
- LinkedIn, job boards, niche sites
- Employee referral program

**8. Activate Outreach Plan**
- Internal referrals
- Passive candidate sourcing (LinkedIn Recruiter, GitHub, etc.)
- Agencies or sourcing partners if needed

---

### **Bucket 3: Screening & Selection**
**9. Screen Resumes**
- Review inbound applications
- Shortlist top candidates for recruiter screen

**10. Conduct Recruiter Screen**
- Assess fit, interest, salary expectations
- Confirm location/availability

**11. Schedule Hiring Manager Interview**
- Deeper skills + experience review
- Cultural fit and role alignment

**12. Coordinate Panel Interviews**
- Technical, behavioural, or case interviews
- Create scorecards and evaluation criteria

**13. Debrief & Select Finalists**
- Panel discussion
- Align on top 1ŌĆō2 candidates

---

### **Bucket 4: Offer & Onboarding**
**14. Conduct References**
- Professional references or background checks

**15. Draft & Extend Offer**
- Customised offer letter
- Verbal and written delivery

**16. Negotiate & Close**
- Address questions, negotiate if needed
- Secure signed offer

**17. Offboard Other Candidates**
- Send thank-yous or rejection messages
- Keep promising profiles warm

**18. Prepare Onboarding Plan**
- Welcome email
- Equipment, accounts, training schedule

---

### **Bucket 5: Post-Hire Follow-Up**
**19. First Week Check-In**
- Confirm start
- Conduct Day 1 and Week 1 touchpoints

**20. 30/60/90-Day Reviews**
- Check-in with manager and employee
- Collect feedback on hiring process

---

### Ō£ģ **Steps to Allow `wise.com` for Iframe Embeds**

#### **Option 1: For SharePoint Online Modern Sites**
Modern SharePoint restricts iframe embedding by domain. You need to **use the Embed web part**, but the source domain must allow itself to be embedded (via its `X-Frame-Options` and `Content-Security-Policy` headers).

- **Good news:** Wise widgets **do allow iframe embedding**.
- **Bad news:** SharePoint blocks if the domain isn't on the **allow list**, depending on tenant settings.

