# Posting tips for best reach

1) Hook lines: keep the first two lines strong and specific so “see more” gets clicked.  
2) Timing: post Tues–Thurs in the morning UK time, then add your first comment within 5 minutes.  
3) Nurture: reply to every genuine comment within an hour where possible to keep the post active.  
4) Tagging: optionally tag the NBI company page and one or two relevant communities, not individuals, to avoid awkwardness with confidential clients.  
5) Reshare cadence: reshare once in 5–7 days with a different hook and one success story or requirement highlight.
