## 1  Editing Principles

* **Active, first-person plural** – “We will launch…” instead of passive or “We’re gonna launch…”.  
* **Plain verbs over hype adjectives** – “demonstrate” beats “prove beyond doubt”.  
* **Short sentences; one idea each** – improves scan-read and investor confidence.  
* **Parallel structure in lists** – each bullet starts with the same part of speech.  
* **Replace slang and contractions** – “We have” rather than “We’ve”; avoid “gonna”.  
* **Logical sign-posting** – use headings and topic sentences so key points surface fast.  
* **No em dash** – use a semicolon or comma for clauses, or break into two sentences.

## 3  Quick Style Guide for the Full Document

* **Headings:** Title Case, bold, size-stepped (H2, H3) for hierarchy.  
* **Lists:** Bullets for unordered items; a two-column table for allocations or timelines.  
* **Numbers:** Use digits plus unit (e.g., **£10 million**); spell out numbers that start a sentence.  
* **Capitalisation:** Product names and proper nouns only.  
* **Punctuation:** Prefer commas or periods; reserve semicolons for closely linked clauses.  
* **Voice:** Confident, factual, never boastful—show, do not tell.

Apply these rules section-by-section and the Q&A will read like a polished investor memo without altering any underlying data.

1.4 Describe any player / creator earning capabilities (“cash out”).
