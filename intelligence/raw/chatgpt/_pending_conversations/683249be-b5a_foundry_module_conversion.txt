4.  **Turn Tracker & Initiative**  
    The D&D HUD integrates the combat tracker (showing whose turn it is, initiative order, jump-to-turn buttons). To mirror that, add a hook on `renderCombatTracker` or `renderCombatantHUD` to inject your own display or buttons that call `game.combat.nextTurn()` and related methods.

5.  **Drag-and-Drop & Macros**  
    A big part of bg3-hud-core is drag-dropping spells or items to the hotbar to create macros. You’ll need to enable dropping from the actor sheet into your hotbar region, probably with `html.find(…) .on("drop", event => handleDrop(event))`, then generate a macro that wraps your FFG roll logic.

6.  **Configurable Layouts & Styles**
