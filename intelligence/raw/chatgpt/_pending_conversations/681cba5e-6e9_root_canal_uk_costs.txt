In the UK, private root-canal fees vary widely by practice, tooth type, specialist involvement and location. Here’s what you can generally expect:

**NHS comparator**  
- On the NHS, root-canal work falls under Band 2 at £75.30 per course of treatment citeturn1view0.  

**General-practice private fees**  
- Many general dentists quote a flat range of **£350 – £600** for routine root-canal treatment citeturn3view0.  
- When broken down by tooth type (which correlates roughly with canal number and complexity):  
  - **Front teeth (incisors/canines)**: £250 – £500 citeturn2view0  
  - **Premolars**: £400 – £700 citeturn2view0  
  - **Molars**: £600 – £1 500 citeturn2view0  

(Some smaller practices report slightly lower “from” fees—e.g. front £275, premolar £349, molar £499 citeturn0search11—but it pays to get a specific quote.)

**Specialist endodontist fees**  
- If you’re referred to a specialist your costs rise substantially:  
  - **Incisor / canine** from £1 250  
  - **Premolar** from £1 350  
  - **Molar** from £1 450 citeturn4view0  

**Additional costs**  
- **Initial consultation**: often £50 – £150  
- **X-rays**: £7 – £50 if not included  
- **Post-treatment crown**: typically £600 – £950 (zirconia or porcelain) citeturn2view0  
- **Follow-up visits**: may incur extra fees  

**What drives price differences?**  
1. **Tooth complexity** – molars have more canals, take longer  
2. **Dentist’s experience** – specialists charge more than generalists  
3. **Location** – central London practices tend to be at the top of the ranges  
4. **Need for retreatment** – re-root canals are more complex  

**Tips for getting the best deal**  
- Always request a detailed written estimate before treatment  
- Ask whether X-rays or follow-ups are included  
- Compare a couple of local practices (and consider a dental-plan provider like Denplan or Bupa Smile to spread cost)  

That should give you a clear sense of what to budget for a private root canal in the UK. If you need further help finding a good local practice or securing finance options, let me know.
