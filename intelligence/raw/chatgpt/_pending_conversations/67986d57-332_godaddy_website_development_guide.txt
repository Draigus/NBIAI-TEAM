### **Building Your NBI Games Consultancy Website with Webflow**
Using **Webflow** is a great choice if you want **high design control** without coding. It allows you to visually build your site while offering flexibility similar to custom development.

---

## **🚀 Steps to Get Started with Webflow**
### **1️⃣ Sign Up & Set Up Your Project**
1. **Go to Webflow** ([webflow.com](https://webflow.com)) and create an account.
2. Click **“New Project”** → Choose **a blank template** (or pick a similar prebuilt template to your PowerPoint).
3. Set your **website name** (e.g., "NBI Games").

---

### **2️⃣ Structure Your Website Based on Your PowerPoint Deck**
Your deck includes **sections that can be converted into Webflow components**:

#### ✅ **Homepage (Hero Section)**
- Large **headline**: “NBI Games Consultancy – Your Premier Partner for Game Studios.”
- Subtext about supporting game development, launch, and live services.
- A **Call-to-Action (CTA)** button: "Get Started" or "Contact Us."
- Background **image or subtle animation**.

#### ✅ **Services Section**
- **Grid or cards layout** listing:
  - Optimized workflows.
  - Leadership guidance.
  - Player experience insights.
  - Architecture & planning.
  - Scalable services.
- Use **icons** and short descriptions.

#### ✅ **Game Development & Studio Specializations**
- Two-column layout for **development** and **studio** services.
- Add **animated hover effects** on service offerings.

#### ✅ **Playtesting Process Section**
- **Step-by-step timeline or process flow**:
  - Define Objective → Study Design → Recruitment → Testing → Analysis → Reporting → Debriefing.
- Use **Webflow’s CMS** to make the steps **dynamic and editable**.

#### ✅ **About Us & Testimonials**
- Team member photos, **brief bios** about expertise.
- Logos of **clients & franchises** you’ve worked with.

#### ✅ **Contact Page**
- **Webflow Form** for leads.
- Google Map embed (optional).
- **Email, social links, and phone number.**

---

### **3️⃣ Customize The Design**
1. **Match Colors & Typography**:
   - Use the **same colors** as your PowerPoint (or define a color palette).
   - Select a **clean, professional font**.

2. **Use Webflow's Grid & Flexbox**
   - Ensure content is **responsive**.
   - Add **section-based layout** with **proper spacing**.

3. **Animations & Interactions**
   - **Subtle hover effects** for service cards.
   - A **fade-in scroll animation** for sections.
   - **Smooth transitions** between pages.

---

### **4️⃣ Set Up CMS for Dynamic Content**
If you plan to update sections like:
- **Game Studios & Clients**
- **Playtesting Processes**
- **Team Members**
Then, **Webflow’s CMS** will allow you to **easily update** these without touching the design.

---

### **5️⃣ Connect to Your GoDaddy Domain**
1. In Webflow, go to **Project Settings → Hosting**.
2. Click **"Add Custom Domain"** and enter **NBIGaming.com**.
3. In **GoDaddy**, go to DNS settings and update:
   - **A Record** → Webflow’s IP addresses.
   - **CNAME** → `proxy-ssl.webflow.com`
4. Click **Publish** in Webflow!

---

### **6️⃣ Final Optimizations Before Launch**
✅ **Mobile Optimization** – Test across devices.  
✅ **SEO Best Practices** – Meta descriptions, page titles, alt text.  
✅ **Speed Optimization** – Compress images, enable lazy loading.  
✅ **Analytics Setup** – Add Google Analytics or Hotjar.  

---
