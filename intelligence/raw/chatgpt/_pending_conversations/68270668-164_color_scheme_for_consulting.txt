as an expert web designer what color scheme should i use for a website focused on organizational consulting 

[ASSISTANT]
For an organizational-consulting site you’ll want a palette that conveys trust, clarity and growth. A classic “blue + neutrals + fresh accent” works very well:

- **Primary (Trust & Stability):** Navy Blue – `#1F3A93`  
- **Secondary (Neutral & Professional):** Cool Gray – `#BDC3C7`  
- **Background (Clean & Airy):** Off-White – `#F2F3F4`  
- **Text (Strong Legibility):** Charcoal – `#2C3E50`  
- **Accent (Growth & Action):** Emerald Green – `#2ECC71`  

**Why this works:**  
- Blue signals reliability and expertise.  
- Grays and off-white keep the layout uncluttered.  
- A deep charcoal ensures clear readability.  
- A green accent highlights “positive change” and draws attention to buttons or links.  

**Usage tips:**  
- Use navy for headers or key panels.  
- Keep most backgrounds off-white, with gray for subtle dividers.  
- Reserve emerald for CTAs or data highlights, and charcoal for body text.  

That combination will feel both authoritative and forward-looking without overwhelming your visitors.
