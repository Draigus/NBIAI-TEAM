Can i perform 1099 work while under a tier 2 visa in the uk

[ASSISTANT]
No, you cannot perform 1099 work (self-employment) while under a Tier 2 visa in the UK. The Tier 2 visa is a work visa that ties you to a specific employer who has sponsored your visa. Self-employment or freelancing is not permitted under this visa category.
