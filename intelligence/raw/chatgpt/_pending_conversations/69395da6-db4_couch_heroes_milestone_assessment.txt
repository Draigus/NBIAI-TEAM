Primary focus

Capacity check  
- This is the busiest milestone in terms of design work.  
- One senior consultant can realistically:
  - Lead 3 to 4 heavy design workshops (org, roles, operating rhythm).  
  - Partner with recruiter and client to tune the hiring loop.  
  - Design the framework structure and definitions.  
- Risk is medium. It is doable if there is strong client participation and decisions are not delayed.

   - Milestones 1 to 3 are heavy but realistic for one senior consultant operating at 2 to 3 days per week, provided:

     - Internal leaders take ownership as soon as possible rather than everything flowing back to the consultant.
