## Funnel Metrics & KPIs (Web2 Focused)  
- **Awareness & Acquisition Metrics:** Track how many potential players you’re reaching and how many convert. - **Activation & Onboarding:** Within the game, gauge the **activation rate** – what percentage of new sign-ups complete the tutorial and play at least one PvP battle (an indicator that they truly became active players). - **Engagement & Retention:** Standard gaming KPIs apply: **Daily Active Users (DAU)** and **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** show the scale of engaged players. Monitor **session length** and **sessions per day** per user to understand engagement depth (are players just logging in briefly or playing long sessions?). Most importantly, track **retention rates** at Day 1, Day 7, Day 30 – e.g., what % of players come back the next day, in a week, in a month. Healthy retention curves (comparative to other strategy games) signal that players find lasting value. For a strategy game, aim for strong D1 and D7 retention by hooking players with social ties (guilds, friends) and long-term goals.  
- **Community & Social KPIs:** Since SARGE emphasizes community, measure community growth and activity. - **Monetization & LTV:** Even with a Web2 focus, revenue matters. Track **ARPDAU** (Average Revenue per Daily Active User) and conversion rate of payers (what % of players make any purchase). Early on (Months 1–3), focus more on retention than maximizing ARPU, but ensure to set up tracking for revenue-related metrics from day one.  
- **Funnel Drop-off Analysis:** At each stage (awareness -> click -> install -> tutorial complete -> 7-day retained), identify drop-offs.
