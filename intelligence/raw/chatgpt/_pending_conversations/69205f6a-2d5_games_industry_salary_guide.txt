ROLE AND MISSION

1. Identify all relevant video game studio roles across disciplines.

   - UK, by region: London, Midlands, Scotland, Ireland

   - EU, by country (all reasonably sized markets with available data)
4. Use as many reputable sources as possible and keep data current to 2025.
5. Deliver:
   - A clearly written overview document

4. Handling sparse data  
   - If a role is rare in a given region and data is thin, explicitly label it as “estimate” and explain the basis, for example extrapolation from neighbouring regions, related roles, or a blend of smaller samples.
