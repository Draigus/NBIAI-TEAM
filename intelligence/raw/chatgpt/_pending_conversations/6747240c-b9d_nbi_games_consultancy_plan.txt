## 1. Executive Summary
NBI Games Consultancy provides industry-leading services to empower game studios with insights, tools, and strategies for success in a highly competitive market. Our expertise spans market research, data architecture, live service management, operational efficiency, game design, and AI/ML implementation. By delivering customized, scalable, and actionable solutions, we enable studios to optimize workflows, enhance player engagement, and maximize revenue.

---

## 2. Company Overview
### **Mission Statement**
To revolutionize the gaming industry by equipping studios with the insights, tools, and strategies they need to thrive in a dynamic and player-focused market.

### **Vision**
To be the most trusted and transformative consultancy for gaming studios worldwide, fostering innovation, efficiency, and creativity.

### **Core Values**
- **Innovation:** Embrace cutting-edge technology and forward-thinking strategies.
- **Collaboration:** Partner with studios to unlock their full potential.
- **Excellence:** Deliver unparalleled expertise and value.
- **Adaptability:** Tailor solutions to each client’s unique challenges and goals.

---

## 3. Market Opportunity
The gaming industry is valued at over $200 billion and continues to grow rapidly. As studios face increasing complexity in live service management, data-driven decision-making, and player retention, NBI Games Consultancy addresses these challenges with targeted, high-value solutions.

### **Market Trends**
- Growth of live-service games and subscription models.
- Increasing reliance on AI/ML for data insights and player personalization.
- Rising demand for operational efficiency and scalable infrastructure.
- Competitive emphasis on user experience and player retention.

---

## 4. Service Offerings

### **Market Research & Analysis**
We provide comprehensive market insights to inform strategic decisions:
- **Custom Addressable Market Analysis:** Identify and quantify opportunities tailored to your studio’s goals.
- **Player Profiling:** Deep insights into player behavior and demographics.
- **Competitor Analysis:** Benchmark against market leaders and identify differentiation opportunities.
- **Forecasting (LTV, Players, Revenue):** Predict revenue and player lifetime value with precision.
- **User Experience (UX):** Optimize player journeys for satisfaction and retention.
- **Segmentation:** Define and target key player groups for maximum impact.

### **Data Systems & Services**
Robust data architecture tailored for scalability and performance:
- **Scalable Cloud Infrastructure:** Seamless growth without bottlenecks.
- **CI/CD Data Architecture:** Continuous integration and deployment pipelines.
- **Robust Telemetry Implementation:** Capture and analyze player data effectively.
- **KPI’s and Goal Setting:** Track and achieve measurable outcomes.
- **Near Real-Time Data:** Rapid decision-making with up-to-date metrics.
- **Studio Self-Service Tools:** Enable teams to leverage data without bottlenecks.
- **Custom & Automated Insights:** Streamline reporting and analysis.

### **Live Service Architecture**
Designing systems to optimize player engagement and revenue:
- **Quantified Roadmapping:** Data-driven development schedules.
- **Economy Modelling:** Balance in-game economies for long-term engagement.
- **Event and Lever Planning:** Create compelling, timely events.
- **Team Structure:** Build effective teams for live service operations.
- **Business Modelling:** Evaluate and optimize revenue streams.
- **Asset Warehouse Planning:** Streamline asset creation and management.

### **Live Service Management**
Operate live games efficiently and profitably:
- **Release Cycle Planning:** Optimize content updates.
- **Communications Planning:** Coordinate messaging with precision.
- **Economy Tuning:** Adjust in-game systems for engagement and profitability.
- **Live Service Forecasting:** Anticipate trends and player behaviors.
- **Balance Tuning:** Refine gameplay mechanics for fairness and fun.
- **AERM Data Implementation:** Advanced Engagement and Retention Metrics integration.

### **Operational Efficiency**
Enhance studio productivity and workflow:
- **Customized Workflows:** Tailored processes for efficiency.
- **Pipeline Assessment & Optimization:** Identify and address bottlenecks.
- **Data-Driven Production:** Leverage analytics to improve project outcomes.
- **Tailored Communication Structure:** Enhance collaboration across teams.
- **Velocity Mapping on Workstreams:** Optimize resource allocation and timelines.
- **Integration Optimization:** Seamlessly incorporate tools and processes.

### **Game Design & Mechanics**
Refine core gameplay to maximize player engagement:
- **Templatized Design Spec Implementation:** Streamline development with proven templates.
- **Core Loop Player Motivation Assessment:** Ensure long-term player satisfaction.
- **Progression Cadence Testing:** Balance progression systems.
- **FTUE (First-Time User Experience) Optimization:** Hook players from the start.
- **Competitive Design Feature Study:** Stay ahead of the competition.

### **AI/ML Strategic Implementation**
Harness AI and ML for innovation and efficiency:
- **Feasibility Study by Use Case:** Evaluate potential applications.
- **AI Governance Framework:** Ensure ethical and effective AI use.
- **AI Data Analyst:** Integrate AI for actionable insights.
- **AI Anomaly Engine:** Detect and address outliers in player data.
- **AI NPCs:** Enhance gameplay with intelligent non-player characters.
- **ML Recommendation Engine:** Personalize player experiences.
- **ML-Driven Forecasting:** Predict trends and optimize strategies.

### **Studio Strategy and Organizational Consulting**
Transformative guidance for sustainable growth:
- **Leadership Development:** Build resilient and visionary leaders.
- **Team Cohesion Programs:** Foster collaboration and efficiency.
- **Scalable Organizational Strategies:** Prepare for growth and industry changes.
