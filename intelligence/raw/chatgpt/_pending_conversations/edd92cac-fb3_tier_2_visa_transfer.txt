How long does it take to tranfer a tier 2 visa from one employer to another in the uk

[ASSISTANT]
Transferring a Tier 2 visa (now known as the Skilled Worker visa) from one employer to another in the UK typically takes up to 8 weeks to receive a decision from the Home Office. During this time, you must not start working for your new employer until your new visa has been granted. If you need a faster decision, you can opt for priority processing, which costs an additional £500 and provides a decision within 5 working days, or super-priority processing for £1000, which provides a decision within 1-2 working days【6†source】【7†source】.

To transfer your visa, you need to:
1. Find a new job with an employer who has a valid Sponsor Licence.
2. Obtain a new Certificate of Sponsorship from your new employer.
3. Submit an online Skilled Worker visa application up to 3 months before starting your new job.
4. Pay the application fee, biometric fee, and healthcare surcharge if applicable.
5. Provide required documents, including proof of meeting the salary and English language requirements【6†source】【8†source】【9†source】.

You can continue working in your current job while your application is being processed, but you cannot start your new job until you receive the new visa approval【9†source】.

can i tbe expedited

[ASSISTANT]
Yes, you can expedite the transfer of your Tier 2 visa (Skilled Worker visa) application. The Home Office offers two priority services for faster processing:

1. **Priority Processing**: For an additional £500, this service provides a decision within 5 working days.
2. **Super-Priority Processing**: For an additional £1000, this service provides a decision within 1-2 working days.

When you apply, you will be informed if these faster processing options are available to you【6†source】【7†source】【9†source】.
