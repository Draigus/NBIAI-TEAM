If I work as an employee at 220,000 gbp annual pay what rate should I charge as a consultant

[ASSISTANT]
To determine a suitable consulting rate based on an annual salary of £220,000, you can use the following steps:

1. **Determine Annual Working Hours:**
   - Typically, a full-time employee works about 2,000 hours per year (assuming 40 hours per week for 50 weeks, allowing for 2 weeks of vacation).

2. **Calculate Hourly Rate:**
   - Divide the annual salary by the annual working hours.
   \[
   \text{Hourly Rate} = \frac{220,000 \text{ GBP}}{2,000 \text{ hours}} = 110 \text{ GBP/hour}
   \]

3. **Account for Additional Costs and Profit:**
   - Consultants typically charge more to cover expenses, benefits, taxes, downtime, and to ensure profit. A common multiplier is between 2 to 3 times the hourly rate.

4. **Calculate Consulting Rate:**
   - Using a multiplier of 2 to 3:
   \[
   \text{Consulting Rate} = 110 \text{ GBP/hour} \times 2 = 220 \text{ GBP/hour}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Consulting Rate} = 110 \text{ GBP/hour} \times 3 = 330 \text{ GBP/hour}
   \]

Based on these calculations, you should charge between **£220 to £330 per hour** as a consultant.

Creating a compelling deck for a video game consultancy involves several key sections. Here’s a structured approach to building an effective deck:

  - Your name and contact information.

- **Service Offerings:**
  - Describe the specific consultancy services you provide (e.g., game design, market analysis, user experience optimization, monetization strategies).
- **Expertise:**
  - Highlight areas of specialization (e.g., mobile games, VR/AR, eSports).
  
### 3. **Market Analysis**
- **Industry Insights:**
  - Present current trends and data in the video game industry.
  - Showcase your understanding of market demands and opportunities.
  
### 4. **Client Success Stories**
- **Case Studies:**
  - Provide examples of past successful projects.
  - Include metrics and testimonials to demonstrate your impact.
  
### 5. **Approach and Methodology**
- **Process:**
  - Outline your consulting process from initial assessment to implementation and follow-up.
  - Explain the tools and frameworks you use.
  
### 6. **Team and Expertise**
- **Team Profiles:**
  - Highlight key members of your team, their backgrounds, and their expertise.
  - Include notable achievements and industry experience.
  
### 7. **Service Packages and Pricing**
- **Packages:**
  - Define different service packages (e.g., basic, standard, premium).
  - Clearly state what each package includes.
- **Pricing:**
  - Provide a transparent pricing model.
  - Consider offering customizable options to fit various budgets.
  
### 8. **Client Testimonials**
- **Feedback:**
  - Include quotes and endorsements from satisfied clients.
  
### 9. **Call to Action**
- **Next Steps:**
  - Encourage potential clients to get in touch.
  - Provide clear instructions on how they can engage your services.
  
### 10. **Contact Information**
- **Details:**
  - Reiterate your contact information.
  - Include your website, email, phone number, and social media links.

### Visual and Design Tips:
- **Consistency:**
  - Use a consistent color scheme and font style that aligns with your brand.
- **Visuals:**
  - Include high-quality images, charts, and infographics to make the deck visually appealing.
- **Clarity:**
  - Keep text concise and to the point.
  - Use bullet points for easy reading.
