# Two effective pre‑launch tactics—compared  
**1) Pre‑registration campaigns**  
- **What it is:** Collect sign‑ups ahead of launch and reward them at day one.  
- **Strengths:** Generates a guaranteed “day‑0” spike; creates FOMO; lets you forecast demand. Case in point: a pre‑reg reward lifted conversions ~20% for FAITH (Nexon). fileciteturn7file123  
- **Watchouts:** Pre‑reg intent can decay—nurture the list (updates, minis, rewards) so sign‑ups actually convert.

**2) Influencer/creator seeding**  
- **What it is:** Give early access/materials to mid‑tier creators for authentic previews, guides, and streams.  
- **Strengths:** Trusted voices drive high‑quality traffic; over half of marketers say influencers bring better customers; scales UGC you can reuse in ads. fileciteturn7file123  
- **Watchouts:** Results vary by creator‑fit; you’ll need tight briefs, clear CTAs, and tracking to prove ROI. fileciteturn7file123

**When to use which?**  
- Choose **pre‑reg** if you need a predictable launch surge and a CRM base to retarget at low cost. fileciteturn7file123  
- Lean on **influencers** if your game demos well on video and you want credibility plus evergreen content for paid UA. fileciteturn7file123
