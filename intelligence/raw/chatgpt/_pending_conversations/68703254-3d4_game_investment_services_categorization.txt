### How to Sell It

* **Modular pricing:** each audit is a fixed-fee work-package with a clear deliverable (scorecard, risk matrix, benchmark report, recovery roadmap).  
* **Staged engagements:** investors typically start with Packages 1-4 for term-sheet diligence, then fund Packages 5-9 once they lead the round or join the board.  
* **Ongoing monitoring retainer:** turn the scorecards into quarterly portfolio “pulse checks” so investors see variance early and can intervene.

### Why Investors Pay

1. **Risk compression:** Independent, domain-expert verification shrinks valuation haircuts and equity buffers.  
2. **Faster go/no-go:** Our packaged scorecards map directly to investment IC rubrics, eliminating back-and-forth.  
3. **Post-close value creation:** Actionable 100-day plans, live-ops roadmaps and KPI dashboards allow investors to track progress against the same metrics used in diligence.

Use this framework in proposals and pitch decks—the language (“Assessment,” “Audit,” “Readiness Review”) mirrors private-equity and venture capital lexicon while clearly linking every spreadsheet line-item to a service investors instinctively recognise and are willing to fund.

### 1 Market Feasibility Assessment  
*Purpose – “How big is the prize and who can we really reach?”*  

- **TAM/SAM model, comparable title benchmarks, scenario workbook**  
- Know segments  
- Addressable market but do they understand subjective operational market (who you can actually get)  
- Are you focusing on operational/reachable segments throughout  
- How crowded is the genre  
- Clear brand presence  
- Economic landscape  

---

### 2 Product Fun & Retention Audit  
*Purpose – “Will players stick around and recommend the game?”*  

- Issue matrix, priority fix list, annotated clip library  
- Is the product engaging/fun/playable  
- Is the game structured for continued engagement and revenue  
- How many hours  
- Does it work  
- Clear game identity, alignment top to bottom  
- Player engagement strategy  
- Design quality review  

---

### 3 Economy & Monetisation Strategy Review  
*Purpose – “Is the in-game economy sound and commercially optimised?”*  

- Economy review, price tests, monetisation features backlog  
- If (live) is it based on a sound economy  
- Will it scale (multiplayer) in fiscally appropriate manner  

---

### 4 Financial Model & Runway Validation  
*Purpose – “Do the numbers add up and is the studio financed to finish?”*  

- Re-built forecast model, sensitivity table, commentary  
- Burn rate or opex ➜ runway… long enough?  
- Overall business model  
- Contracts – percentage of revenue for other investors  
- Investor/other investor contract requirements and terms  

---

### 5 Production Plan & Delivery Risk Audit  
*Purpose – “Can they actually ship when they say they will?”*  

- Risk register, revised Gantt, 100-day recovery plan  
- Five-year content pipeline plan  
- Strong project management method  
- Standardised nomenclature for product and project elements  
- Op & tracking systems  
- Accuracy of planned opex per milestone  
- Anticipated timing of launch  
- Minimal scope creep  
- Gate/milestone structure and adherence  
- Clear systems quality review  

---

### 6 Live-Ops & Content Readiness Assessment  
*Purpose – “Can the team run a stable, evergreen live service?”*  

- Readiness scorecard, event cadence roadmap, capex roadmap  
- Will it scale (multiplayer)… _(in live-ops context)_  
- Five-year content pipeline plan _(cross-links with Production)_  

---

### 7 Data-Pipeline & KPI Capability Review  
*Purpose – “Will decisions be driven by the right telemetry?”*  

- Telemetry schema, map, maturity matrix, analytics roadmap  
- KPI dashboard specification

### 8 Go-to-Market & UA Stress-Test  
*Purpose – “Can they acquire, convert and retain users at target CAC/ROAS?”*  

- Channel plan, CAC model, creative test backlog

### 9 Community & Player-Support Readiness

### 10 Organisational Health & Governance Due-Diligence  
*Purpose – “Is the studio culture, structure and leadership fit for growth?”*
