[ASSISTANT]
**Strengths**  
- **Comprehensive scope**: Clearly covers the full “development → launch → live” journey, showing you understand a studio’s end‑to‑end needs.  
- **Expertise signal**: Phrases like “cutting‑edge strategies” and “deep industry expertise” reassure clients you’re both innovative and seasoned.  
- **Outcome focus**: “Empower studios to create, launch, and sustain market‑leading titles” puts the client’s success front and center.

---

**Areas to sharpen**  
1. **Brevity**: It reads as a paragraph rather than a punchy tagline.  
2. **Differentiation**: Many consultancies promise “market‑leading” outcomes—what makes NBI unique?  
3. **Avoid redundancy**: “Create, launch, and sustain” echoes the earlier “development, launch and live”; pick one triplet.

---

**Suggested taglines**  
- “NBI Games: Your end‑to‑end partner for building, launching and growing standout titles.”  
- “From prototype to live ops, NBI Games delivers cutting‑edge strategy and proven expertise.”  
- “Empowering studios with the strategy and support to craft, launch and sustain top‑tier games.”
