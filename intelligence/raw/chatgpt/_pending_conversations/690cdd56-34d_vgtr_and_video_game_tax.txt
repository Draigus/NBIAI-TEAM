what is EEA

[ASSISTANT]
EEA means the **European Economic Area**. It combines all **EU countries** with **Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Norway** into a single market for goods, services, people, and capital.  
Notes for your tax context: the **UK is not in the EEA**, and **Switzerland is not EEA** either. Under the old VGTR rules, ŌĆ£EEA spendŌĆØ meant costs on work done in any EU country or in Iceland, Liechtenstein, or Norway.
