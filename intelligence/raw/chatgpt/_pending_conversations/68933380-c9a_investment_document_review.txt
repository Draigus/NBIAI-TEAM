**Executive summary**

Your “Riley’s Q & A” deck is overflowing with passion and ambition, but an institutional investor will scan for three things first: (i) crisp evidence that the opportunity is big and reachable, (ii) a realistic, bottoms-up plan to get there, and (iii) clear-eyed risk disclosure with mitigations. At present the document *tells* rather than *shows* on those points, and the confident, almost promotional tone sometimes risks credibility. Below is a section-by-section critique and concrete rewrite guidance.

| **Feature depth** (e.g., 280+ assets, 4,300 upgrades) suggests content moat. fileciteturn0file0 | Investors will immediately ask “how many of these are art-complete and balanced?” Include a simple progress bar or %-complete table to pre-empt. |
