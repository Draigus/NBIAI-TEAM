What are the itemized costs for an employer to support 1 skilled worker visa with a spouse and child

[ASSISTANT]
For a UK employer to sponsor a Skilled Worker visa (Tier 2) for one individual, along with a spouse and child as dependents, the costs generally include:

### 1. **Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)**
   - **Cost**: £199 per worker.

### 2. **Immigration Skills Charge (ISC)**
   - **Cost**: £364 per year (for small/charity sponsors) or £1,000 per year (for medium/large sponsors). 
   - This applies to the main worker only (not dependents).
   - Example for a 3-year visa: £1,092 for a small sponsor or £3,000 for a large sponsor.

### 3. **Visa Application Fees (for each applicant)**
   - **Main Worker**:
     - Up to 3 years: £719.
     - More than 3 years: £1,423.
   - **Spouse and Child** (same cost as main worker per person):
     - Up to 3 years: £719 per dependent.
     - More than 3 years: £1,423 per dependent.
   - Example for 3 years for a family of three: £719 x 3 = £2,157.

### 4. **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)**
   - **Cost**: £624 per person per year.
   - This is for the worker, spouse, and child.
   - Example for 3 years for a family of three: £624 x 3 x 3 = £5,616.

### Total Example for a 3-Year Visa:
- **Small Employer (ISC £1,092)**:  
  CoS + ISC + Visa Fees + IHS = £199 + £1,092 + £2,157 + £5,616 = **£9,064**.
  
- **Large Employer (ISC £3,000)**:  
  CoS + ISC + Visa Fees + IHS = £199 + £3,000 + £2,157 + £5,616 = **£10,972**.

These are approximate figures and can vary based on specific visa types, durations, and exemptions.

What is the cumulative cost

[ASSISTANT]
The cumulative cost for an employer to support one Skilled Worker visa with a spouse and child, assuming a 3-year visa term, is as follows:

### Small Employer:
- **Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)**: £199
- **Immigration Skills Charge (ISC)**: £1,092
- **Visa Fees**: £2,157
- **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)**: £5,616

**Total (Small Employer)**: £9,064

### Large Employer:
- **Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)**: £199
- **Immigration Skills Charge (ISC)**: £3,000
- **Visa Fees**: £2,157
- **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)**: £5,616

**Total (Large Employer)**: £10,972

So, the cumulative cost would be **£9,064** for a small employer and **£10,972** for a large employer, over a 3-year period.
