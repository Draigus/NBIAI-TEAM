- Three years of title-level P&L, revenue by platform and region, cohort waterfalls, LTV to CAC by channel, payback charts, churn and reactivation analysis.  
- UA incrementality and MMM or experiment readouts, creator and UGC contribution metrics.

**Operating model**
- 36-month base/bear/bull with drivers named and sensitivities for platform fees, currency, and CPI, plus cash conversion cycle.

**Organisation and operations**
- Org design with shared services, content pipeline cadence, dev velocity metrics, backlog burn-down, vendor and licence map.

**Controls and governance**
- Monthly reporting pack templates, KPI glossary, BI access with view-only roles.

**Acceptance tests**
- Historic actuals reconcile to audited or management accounts.  
- LTV model recreates a published cohort within one percentage point by month six.

## Venture debt
**Credit file**
- Latest equity round docs and investor letters, board consent for debt, cash runway analysis, covenant model with headroom by month, 13-week cash flow, AR ageing, revenue backlog, pipeline.  
- Security package: IP list with jurisdictions, lien and litigation searches, insurance, key-person coverage.

**Reporting**
- Monthly covenant and KPI reporting template, notification covenants calendar.

**Acceptance tests**
- Covenant model shows minimum six months headroom in base case.  
- Security filings match IP list and no prior liens exist.

## Private equity buyout
**Sell-side pack**
- Vendor due diligence or QoE, audited financials or carve-out statements, legal DD index, labour and HR DD, tax DD, IT and cyber DD.

**Commercial**
- Title performance tree with margin by content type, slate focus plan, M&A pipeline with synergy cases, cost take-out model and phased integration plan.

**Technical**
- Deep tech DD: engine and toolchain map, build farm and CI, cloud cost breakdown, incident history, compliance and security certifications.

**Contracts**
- Platform, distribution, licensing, and key vendor contracts with change-of-control clauses summarised.

**Acceptance tests**
- QoE ties to management accounts within stated adjustments.  
- Integration plan has accountable owners, milestones, and validated synergy assumptions.

## Strategic or corporate development
**Strategic fit**
- Fit memo mapping gaps filled in portfolio, cross-media options, regional expansion, and UGC or creator leverage.

**Integration blueprint**
- Product and tech integration plan, data integration and privacy impact assessment, talent retention and earn-out design, comms plan.

**Regulatory**
- Antitrust counsel note, foreign investment review considerations, exclusivity and platform neutrality analysis.

**Acceptance tests**
- Red-flag list has mitigation owners and timelines.  
- Retention plan covers the top decile of creators or engineers with numbers.

**Acceptance tests**

- FX hedging policy documented with triggers.
