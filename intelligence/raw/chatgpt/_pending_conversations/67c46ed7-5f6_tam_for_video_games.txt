To determine the **Total Addressable Market (TAM)** for a video game, the following key dimensions should be analyzed:

### **1. Platform-Based Market Size**
   - **PC Gaming**: Steam, Epic Games Store, GOG, Microsoft Store, etc.
   - **Console Gaming**: PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch.
   - **Mobile Gaming**: iOS (Apple App Store), Android (Google Play).
   - **Cloud Gaming**: Xbox Cloud, PlayStation Now, Nvidia GeForce Now, Google Stadia (if applicable).
   - **VR/AR Gaming**: Meta Quest, PlayStation VR, HTC Vive.

### **2. Genre Demand & Market Segmentation**
   - Shooter (FPS/TPS)
   - Role-Playing Games (RPGs, MMORPGs)
   - Action-Adventure
   - Strategy (RTS, Turn-Based)
   - Battle Royale
   - Sports & Racing
   - Simulation & Casual Games
   - Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA)
   - Puzzle & Casual Games
   - Card & Board Games
   - Horror & Survival

### **3. Geographic Market Potential**
   - **North America (USA, Canada)**
   - **Europe (Germany, UK, France, Nordics, etc.)**
   - **Asia-Pacific (China, Japan, South Korea, India)**
   - **Latin America (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina)**
   - **Middle East & Africa**
   - **Oceania (Australia, New Zealand)**

### **4. Player Demographics**
   - **Age Groups**: Teens (13-18), Young Adults (18-35), Adults (35+)
   - **Gender Distribution**: Male, Female, Non-binary
   - **Gaming Preferences**: Hardcore, Casual, Social
   - **Spending Habits**: Free-to-Play (F2P) Players, Premium Players, Whales (High-Spenders)

### **5. Business Model & Monetization Scope**
   - **Premium (One-time Purchase)**
   - **Free-to-Play (F2P) with Microtransactions**
   - **Subscription-Based (Game Pass, PlayStation Plus, Apple Arcade)**
   - **In-Game Monetization (DLCs, Season Passes, Cosmetics, Loot Boxes)**
   - **Advertising Revenue (Mobile & Free Games)**
   - **NFT/Web3 Gaming Economy (if applicable)**

### **6. Competitive Market & Similar Game Benchmarks**
   - Market positioning vs. existing top-performing games.
   - Historical sales data of similar genre & platform titles.
   - Audience size of competing franchises.

### **7. Player Engagement & Lifetime Value (LTV)**
   - **Daily Active Users (DAU)**
   - **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**
   - **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**
   - **Retention Rates (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30)**
   - **Churn Rates**
   - **Average Playtime Per User**

### **8. Localization & Accessibility**
   - Potential audience expansion through multilingual support.
   - Localization trends (e.g., high demand for translated games in China, Japan, Germany, etc.).

### **9. Esports & Streaming Potential**
   - Viability in the esports ecosystem.
   - Popularity on streaming platforms (Twitch, YouTube Gaming, Facebook Gaming).
   - Sponsorship & tournament monetization opportunities.

### **10. Hardware & Technological Considerations**
   - **Hardware Adoption Rates**: Percentage of gamers with high-end PCs, next-gen consoles.
   - **System Requirements & Performance Optimization**: Can the game reach lower-end devices for broader adoption?
   - **Cloud Gaming Accessibility**: Expanding to markets with limited hardware but strong internet infrastructure.

These dimensions collectively define the potential **Total Addressable Market (TAM)** for a video game, guiding strategic decisions in development, marketing, and monetization【15†source】.
