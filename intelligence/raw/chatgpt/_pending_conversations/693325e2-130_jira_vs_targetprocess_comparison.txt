- Pricing  
  - Jira Cloud has a free plan for up to 10 users, which already includes core project tracking. citeturn0search15  
  - Standard cloud pricing for small teams in 2025 is roughly 8.60 USD per user per month, with Premium around 17 USD per user per month, with some variation and increases announced for late 2025. citeturn0search19turn0search23turn0search28

Targetprocess  

- Pricing  
  - Targetprocess is positioned and priced as an enterprise agile portfolio management tool. Public pricing examples show annual fees around 40,500 USD for a 25 user package on AWS Marketplace, which includes a mix of full and connected users. citeturn0search12turn0search16  
  - Documentation and G Cloud listings emphasise minimum user counts of 10 and a multi component pricing model (full users, connected users, limited users, private cloud), which is materially more expensive than Jira on a per seat basis. citeturn0search1turn0search16turn0search29  

Summary on cost and scale  

- For a 10 to 20 person consultancy, Jira is orders of magnitude cheaper and has a viable free tier.  
- Targetprocess makes financial sense when you are running a sizeable portfolio with dozens to hundreds of users and need enterprise level portfolio and SAFe coverage.
