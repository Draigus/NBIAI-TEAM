### 🔒 Why This Limitation Exists
OpenAI Agents currently:
- **Cannot run headless browsers or access authenticated web apps**
- **Cannot SSH, RDP, or VPN into your machine**
- Cannot run persistent background processes or hold live sessions with local servers
- Must operate **within a secure sandbox** that restricts outbound network activity

So even with a deployed ChatGPT Agent, **you must provide the data explicitly** (files, URLs to public manifests, exported configs, etc.).
