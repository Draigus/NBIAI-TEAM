**Premise:** Cara returns to the Ruins of Lorkaj to read what the city still holds. She must decide which truth to bring back to the people and which to leave in the dark.

**Chapters**
1. **Gate of the Fallen Name**  
She passes under the carved warning and feels the old city’s hunger for power that is not hers to claim.  
2. **Murals and Dust**  
Deep chambers show a method that ate magic to build order. The air tastes like iron and ash.  
3. **A Line in Stone**  
A floor of names reveals which houses fed the city and which were fed to it.  
4. **A Test of Silence**  
A chamber answers power with power. Cara chooses restraint and lives.  
5. **The True Cost**  
Records speak of children taken as measures, then discarded. Rage must be yoked to justice, not ruin.  
6. **What to Carry Home**  
She leaves with enough to warn and to teach, and without the one thing that would tempt a new Lorkaj to rise.
