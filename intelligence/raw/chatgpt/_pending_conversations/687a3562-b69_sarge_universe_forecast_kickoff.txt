### 🧭 Project Purpose  
To build a robust, reusable, and scalable 3-year forecast system for the Sarge Universe game ecosystem. The system must integrate telemetry, monetization logic, content scheduling, player economy inputs, and retention curves to enable interactive simulations and live-updated projections for key stakeholders (Product, Finance, Marketing, Exec).

The output must be productionalized in agentic workflows and surfaced via a **lovable, accessible front-end** designed for internal and investor use.

---

### 📦 Core Features of the Forecast System  

| Feature                         | Description                                                                                                                                              |
|---------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Revenue Modeling Engine**     | Multi-channel forecasting across IAPs, P2E economy, player-led tournament income, and UGC monetization.                                                 |
| **Retention & Session Curves**  | Models built from similar titles (Clash of Clans, Boom Beach, Last War) and tuned to Sarge's platform dynamics (Telegram, Web3, PvP-driven loops).     |
| **MTX & Content Pipelines**     | Configurable system for simulating new event/item/shop pipelines, including pricing strategy, event cadence, and content velocity.                     |
| **User Segmentation**           | Forecast by player cohorts (spenders, whales, free players, hosts) and funnel stages.                                                                    |
| **Currency Economy Simulator**  | Inflows/outflows (faucet/sink) modeling across soft currency, STARS, tournament tokens, and PvP rewards.                                                |
| **Scenario Planner (Agentic)**  | Auto-adjusts models based on parameter changes (e.g., new unit release, economy adjustment, or UA spikes).                                               |
| **Dashboard Front-End**         | Interactive frontend with toggleable views by role (CEO, PM, LiveOps, Marketing) with scenario input forms and quick-snapshot visuals.                   |

   - How many unique SKUs are live at any time?

   - What is the actual cadence of content?  
   - Will we drop 1 map/unit/month? How often do PvE/PvP events rotate?

3. **Currency Systems**  
   - What are the **major faucets and sinks** of each soft/hard currency?  
   - Are there inflation/deflation mechanics?

   - Is there a BP system, and if so, what’s the tiered spend and duration?  
   - Any gacha mechanics or randomized rewards?

5. **Retention Expectations**  
   - What is the estimated D1, D7, D30 retention by player type (F2P, P2E, whale)?  
   - Do we have a PvP drop-off curve?

6. **UGC Forecasting**  
   - How many player-created tournaments are expected per day/week?  
   - What’s the average tournament fee, rake %?

7. **Platform Distribution**  
   - Telegram only or Web/iOS later?  
   - Should the model support transition to mobile store installs?

8. **Monetization Ceiling**  
   - What is the expected ARPU, ARPPU, and conversion % at scale?

9. **KPIs for Success**  
   - What internal KPIs must this forecast support?  
   - Is it designed to drive marketing budget, investor narratives, or production planning?
