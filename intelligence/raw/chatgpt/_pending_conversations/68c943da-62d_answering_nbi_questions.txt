## Target Client Profile

**Size**  
- Primary: mid-size and scale-up studios or publishers with 50 to 500 staff and active titles in development or live service.  
- Secondary: AAA units or labels seeking focused help on organisational health, product analytics, or portfolio planning.  
- Tertiary: funded indies graduating to their first professional pipeline who need senior scaffolding for data, operations, or GTM.

**Type**  
- Developers and publishers building or running PC, console, and mobile titles.  
- Investor and PE funds that require diligence, forecasting, and portfolio operating plans.  
- Select tech providers in tooling, analytics, or back-end who need go-to-market and customer success design for games clients.

**Geography**  
- UK and EU core.  
- North America for growth mandates, diligence, and executive advisory.  
- Remote-first delivery with on-site intensives for workshops, audits, and leadership work.

## Differentiators

- **Veteran operators, not slide-ware.** Senior team with ship history and P&L responsibility across live service, analytics, and studio ops.  
- **Decision-backed analytics.** We start from the decisions leaders must make, then design the evidence and systems to answer them clearly.  
- **Organisational health as a performance system.** Leadership behaviours, rituals, and metrics tied directly to delivery speed, quality, and financial outcomes.  
- **Integrated stack thinking.** Product, people, process, and data designed together, so dashboards and rituals change decisions within weeks, not months.  
- **Reusable IP and templates.** Playbooks for forecasting, KPI taxonomies, org diagnostics, and delivery cadences that compress time to value.  
- **Hands-on build.** We do the audits and the fixes. We implement pipelines, dashboards, hiring plans, and operating rhythms alongside the client.

## Service Breakdown and Deliverables

**1) Data Systems and Analytics**  
- Discovery and metric canon: KPI dictionary, event schema, governance rules.  
- Pipeline design: ingestion, modelling, QA checks, SLAs.  
- Dashboards and reporting: executive, product, and live-ops views with decision prompts.  
- Predictive and scenario models: retention, monetisation, forecasting, cohort dynamics.  
- Experiments: A/B frameworks, guardrails, decision logs.  
- Deliverables: KPI taxonomy, event schema, data architecture diagram, dashboard suite, forecasting workbook, experimentation playbook, runbook, ownership map.

**2) Organisational Health and Operating Model**  
- Leadership and team diagnostics: interviews, survey, behavioural mapping.  
- Operating cadence: ceremonies, artefacts, decision rights, RACI.  
- Performance system: goals, lead and lag metrics, scorecards, review rhythm.  
- Role clarity and headcount plan: team structure, spans and layers, hiring plan.  
- Leadership coaching and enablement: feedback loops, conflict resolution, decision quality.  
- Deliverables: Org health report, operating model blueprint, role catalogue, RACI, scorecards, leadership action plan, coaching schedule.

**3) Product Strategy and Live-Ops**  
- Market and competitor analysis: positioning, feature ladders, content cadence.  
- Product vision and north star metrics.  
- Roadmap and release plan: scope by phase, resourcing, risk register.  
- Monetisation model: economy design principles, pricing tests, revenue scenarios.  
- Live-ops calendar and playbooks: events, offers, content pipelines, KPIs.  
- Deliverables: Strategy memo, market brief, roadmap, live-ops calendar, monetisation model, risk and mitigation plan.

**4) Diligence and Investment Support**  
- Product and studio audit: build state, tech, data, team, and process.  
- KPI quality and forecast review: model reconstruction and sensitivity.  
- Value creation plan for post-close.  
- Deliverables: diligence report, forecast pack, 100-day plan.

## Engagement Models

- **Project**: fixed-scope audits, builds, or strategy packages, typically 4 to 12 weeks with defined artefacts and acceptance tests.  
- **Retainer**: ongoing advisory, performance cadence, and roadmap stewardship with monthly outcomes and a rolling backlog.  
- **Embedded**: fractional leadership or specialist pods working inside client teams to stand up systems or ship critical milestones.  
- **Typical phases**: Discover and define, design and pilot, implement and train, stabilise and handover, then iterate on a quarterly cadence.

## Success Metrics and KPIs

**For clients**  
- Decision latency: time from question to supported decision.  
- Delivery predictability: plan versus actual for milestones and scope.  
- Quality: defect escape rate, incident volume, rework percentage.  
- Product health: retention curves, conversion, ARPDAU, LTV to CAC, content cadence hit rate.  
- Org health: leadership ritual adherence, engagement, attrition, hiring lead time, time to effectiveness for new hires.

**For NBI delivery**  
- Time to first decision-changing dashboard or ritual.  
- Adoption rate of dashboards and operating ceremonies.  
- Cycle time to resolve top three systemic blockers identified in discovery.  
- ROI proxy: uplift in target KPIs tied to implemented recommendations within 90 days of go-live.

## Tone and Style

- **Voice**: senior, plain-spoken, and buyer-aware. Confident without hype.  
- **Register**: professional and accessible. Technical when needed, but always anchored to decisions and outcomes.  
- **Style**: short sentences, strong verbs, scannable structure. Promise, proof, action.  
- **Proof**: case-style evidence and before-after metrics where permitted.  
- **Accessibility**: clear headings, consistent terms, no jargon without definitions.
