# Data Room Audit Methodology and Master Prompt

## Key Content
Seven-role investor committee simulation for data room audit: Partner (Games VC), Mobile F2P Monetization Lead, UA and Growth Lead, Product and Live Ops Lead, Tech and Platform Lead, Finance Lead, Legal and Compliance Lead. Operating procedure: Step 0 creates Data Room Index listing every file with type, date, purpose, and missing standard artifacts. Step 1 does document-by-document line audit: label each claim as Fact/Assumption/Projection/Opinion, record every numeric claim with value/unit/timeframe/definition/source/reconciliation status, force single definitions for ambiguous terms (DAU, MAU, retention, LTV, CPI). Section scorecards score five dimensions: clarity/completeness, numeric integrity, assumption realism, evidence strength, investor relevance. Step 2 creates Cross-Document Reconciliation Matrix checking same metrics across files. Each document gets top 5 risks, top 5 fixes, and "diligence traps" that trigger investor distrust.

## Decisions / Insights
- Glen decided: data room audits must distinguish verified-within-data-room vs internally-inconsistent vs unverifiable claims
- Glen decided: every document section gets individual scores, not just document-level ratings
- Glen decided: "diligence traps" (things that trigger fast investor distrust) must be called out separately from general issues
- Glen observed: ambiguous KPI definitions (what counts as MAU, how retention is measured) are a common cross-document consistency failure
