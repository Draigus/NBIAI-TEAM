# Org Design Assessment and Alternatives for Game Studio

## Key Content
Five structural issues in Couch Heroes org chart: (A) Producer mis-parented through Finance/Ops instead of studio delivery leader; EP disconnected from core build with only QA and Writing. (B) Platform treated as ops subteam when it should have GM-style product ownership. (C) CTO span too flat with no engineering managers shown; CTO becomes bottleneck. (D) Tech Art/VFX in grey zone between Engineering and Art without explicit matrix reporting. (E) Content disciplines (sound, writing) not cleanly grouped under Game Director.

Three alternative designs: Alternative 1 (Classic Functional with strong EP hub) best for early-stage teams under 60 people needing speed. Alternative 2 (Pod/Strike Team model) best for parallel workstreams with clear boundaries. Alternative 3 (Platform+Game dual-track with shared services) best when platform is genuinely a strategic product, not just infrastructure.

## Decisions / Insights
- Glen observed: production should be accountable to studio delivery leader (EP), not Finance; Finance is a partner, not the chain of command
- Glen observed: platform team under Finance/Ops is a red flag when platform is central to strategy
- Glen observed: Game Director should own full player-facing content stack (design, narrative, audio, UX content)
