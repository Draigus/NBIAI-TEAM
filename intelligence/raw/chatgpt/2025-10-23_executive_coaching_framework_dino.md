# Executive Coaching Framework for Senior Game Studio Leader

## Key Content
90-day coaching workstream for a Head of Strategy at a game studio with five capability areas: constructive conflict in roadmap forums, inquiry over certainty in partner decisions, ownership and repair when missteps affect other disciplines, remit discipline via agreed governance, and perspective-taking before action. Instrumentation: decision latency (request to decision), reopen rate within one sprint, escalation count outside governance, meeting artefact completeness. Cadence: weekly 45-min 1:1, fortnightly 30-min CEO sponsor sync, two live observations per month with 24-hour written debrief. Deliverables: coaching plan with three behaviour OKRs, Decision Rights Map aligned to RACI, Decision Log template, Conflict Playbook (Describe > Test assumptions > Options > Decide > Commit), Slow-the-decision checklist, 30/60/90 reports with metric deltas. Acceptance tests include: three high-stakes disagreements resolved without follow-on escalation, two written post-mortems naming personal error, zero remit bypasses over eight weeks.

## Decisions / Insights

- Glen observed: need relapse protocol and taper plan post-90 days to prevent backslide
