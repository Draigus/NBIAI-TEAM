# F2P Game Beta Community Sentiment Analysis (509 Respondents)

## Key Content

Community sentiment from 509 beta respondents for a F2P competitive football game. Strengths: input responsiveness 3.90/5 (76% positive), economic fairness/no P2W 3.76/5 (66% positive), Knockout mode 3.83/5 (highest mode score), unique player appeal 3.72/5, aging strategy 3.68/5 (62% accept). Critical friction: defensive AI 2.54/5 (LOWEST metric, 50.9% negative -- CBs split, defenders abandon position, midfielders fail shape), passing system cited by 203/509 (39.9%) as top disappointment (wrong target, wrong direction, too slow), stability 36 crash/freeze bugs (15% of all bugs). Top feature requests: Pro Clubs/5v5 Ranked (27 upvotes + 80+ survey mentions), Passing Assistance Slider (24 upvotes + 203 survey), 5v5 Ranked Queue with Rewards (22 upvotes + 40+ survey), Custom Tournament Mode (16 upvotes), In-Game Transfer Market (12 upvotes + 30+ survey). NPS +24.6 (40% promoters, 15.5% detractors). Recommendation score 8.0/10. Crossplay 82.5% stable. Community P2W red line: any move toward paid competitive advantage triggers immediate churn.
