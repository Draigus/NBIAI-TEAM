# PlaySage Product Definition and Commercial Strategy

## Key Content

PlaySage is NBI's gaming industry intelligence SaaS (ON HOLD, Glen is sole builder, client work takes priority). 10 modules: Market Overview/TAM, Competitive Landscape, Sentiment Analysis, Foresight (forecasting), Market Watch, Alerts, The Sage (recommendation engine -- the differentiator), Executive Dashboard, Finance/IAP Intelligence, API/Integrations. Key architectural moat: "Cascade Engine" -- cross-module integration intelligence (when MAU forecast drops, automatically checks sentiment, competitor launches, event cadence). Tech stack locked: Next.js App Router, Tailwind + shadcn/ui, Supabase PostgreSQL, Vercel.
