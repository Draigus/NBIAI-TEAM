# Couch Heroes Org Structure (Pre-Offsite, April 2026)

## Key Content

Three-column C-suite structure. CEO/CD (Aris) with COO, CPO (Glen, fractional), CTO (vacant). COO column: HR, Finance, IT, Legal/Compliance, PM. CPO column: Marketing, Brand, PR, Community, BD, Web, plus Executive Producer (Graham, ~May 18 start) overseeing four discipline tracks (QA, Audio, Art, Design) each with embedded Producer. CTO column: Technical Director with senior IC beneath, engineering Lead with Backend eng, Leads group with Client eng. Producer overlay (violet on Miro) is production-management overlay, not line management. CTO vacancy is the most critical hire (Cam/Neil/Nia search restarted Q1 2026).

## Decisions / Insights

- Glen decided: CPO oversees both product (marketing, BD, web) and production (via Exec Producer) functions
- Glen observed: producer overlay is embedded in each discipline, not a separate production department
- Glen identified: CTO vacancy is the highest-pain leadership gap; search restarted Q1 2026
- Glen noted: structure is "likely" pre-offsite; offsite (Apr 27-30) would finalise

## Context

Captured from Glen's Miro board 2026-04-26 for Couch Heroes (~55 employees UK+Greece). Status at time of capture was pre-offsite draft subject to confirmation. Graham (Exec Producer) had accepted offer with ~May 18 start date.
