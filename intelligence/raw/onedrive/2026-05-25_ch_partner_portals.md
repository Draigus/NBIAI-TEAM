# Couch Heroes Partner Portals Design — Diegetic Cross-Game Integration

## Key Content

Partner Portals are CH's core differentiator: other studios' games appear as physical in-world objects (dimensional rifts, arcade cabinets, shops) that players walk up to and step through. Not a launcher or menu. The byte-punk aesthetic provides lore justification (world built on AI proving ground, dimensional fabric is inherently thin). Six portal types: Dimensional Portal (10-60 min, full cinematic transition), Arcade Cabinet (2-15 min, screen zoom), Partner Shop (1-5 min, browse), User Space Portal (variable, personal), Guild Portal (variable, group transition), Hidden Rift (variable, discovery-first). Discovery via Hero Gadget detecting "dimensional signatures" with audio/visual cues rewarding exploration. Entry is a 4-beat signature moment (signal, approach, reveal, invitation). Return is a "homecoming" emphasising CH as the player's home. Revenue model: partner-themed cosmetics, portal reputation, portal mastery marks. Key creative constraints: portals must be diegetic (never menu entries), voluntary (never required for main progression), and quality-gated.

## Decisions / Insights

- Glen/NBI decided: portals are content, never advertisements; players want to enter, not feel sold to
- Glen/NBI decided: partner content keeps its own art style inside the portal; CH frames but doesn't override
- Glen/NBI decided: no CH story quest should gate behind partner portal completion
- Glen/NBI identified: 7 open creative questions including corruption interaction with portals, portal permanence when deals end, and User Space portal limits (recommended 3-5)
- Glen/NBI observed: reference points are Roblox (seamless transitions), Kingdom Hearts (respectful IP integration), Animal Crossing (social visiting)

## Context

Creative brief dated 2026-05-11, owned by Creative Director function. Couch Heroes is a byte-punk cosy MMORPG with F2P cosmetic monetisation. The partner portal system is the primary BD revenue mechanism beyond base game cosmetics.
