# F2P Regional Pricing Matrix — 40+ Countries Across 4 Platforms

## Key Content

Global pricing matrix for a F2P football game covering 40+ countries across Sony, Xbox, Steam, and Epic. Benchmarked against EA FC and Arc Raiders per region. Currency-specific pricing for 30+ currencies including EUR, GBP, AUD, NZD, PLN, CHF, NOK, DKK, SEK, CZK, ZAR, UAH, TRY, ILS, RON, HUF, ARS, BRL, JPY, MXN, KRW, HKD, MYR, THB, TWD, IDR, SGD, CAD, VND, PHP, CNY, INR. Regional adjustment flags: Poland, South Africa, UAE, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Hungary, Singapore flagged for downward adjustment (~5%); Japan, South Korea flagged for upward adjustment (~-5%); Brazil, Malaysia, Thailand flagged for further review (~-15%); Ukraine, India, Indonesia priced intentionally below exchange rate. This matrix goes beyond simple FX conversion to purchasing-power-adjusted regional pricing.

## Decisions / Insights

- NBI decided: regional pricing must go beyond FX conversion to purchasing power parity adjustments
- NBI observed: Gulf states (UAE, Kuwait, Saudi, Qatar) typically overprice relative to purchasing power
- NBI observed: Japan and South Korea can sustain higher prices relative to FX rate
- NBI decided: intentional below-FX pricing for Ukraine, India, Indonesia is an accessibility decision, not an error
- NBI observed: platform-specific pricing (Sony vs Xbox vs Steam vs Epic) creates arbitrage risk if not aligned

## Context

Created as part of GOALS AB SOW 1 pricing benchmarking engagement (100K SEK). Matrix was the core deliverable for platform submission guidance. Deadline was 27 April 2026 (Sony/Xbox pricing review ~9 days before 14 May launch).

## Applicability

- Relevant when: building regional pricing matrices for F2P game launches
- Relevant when: advising on purchasing power parity adjustments beyond FX conversion
- Relevant when: identifying cross-platform pricing arbitrage risks
- Relevant when: submitting pricing to Sony, Xbox, Steam, Epic store systems
