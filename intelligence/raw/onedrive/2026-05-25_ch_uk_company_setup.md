# UK Company Setup Compliance Requirements for Game Studios

## Key Content

NBI delivered a guidance document (originally 24 March 2026, revised 26 March 2026) covering UK company setup requirements for a Greek-headquartered game studio establishing UK entity. Key requirements: Employers' Liability Insurance mandatory (min GBP 5M cover, GBP 2,500/day fine if uninsured, Employers' Liability (Compulsory Insurance) Act 1969, required even for remote/part-time staff, costs GBP 250-800/year for small employer). Must display EL certificate where employees can access it. Workplace pension: auto-enrolment mandatory for any employer with at least one worker, must be registered UK scheme, employees join without barriers, no prohibited charges. Document was NBI UK entity deliverable (NBI Analytics UK Limited) with confidentiality notice.
