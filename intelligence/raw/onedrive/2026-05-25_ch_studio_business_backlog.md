# Game Studio GTM and BD Backlog Template (60+ Items)

## Key Content

Comprehensive GTM and BD backlog for a pre-launch MMORPG studio. GTM sub-feature covers: 4 player segment ICPs (Explorer primary, Socialiser secondary, Achiever tertiary, Killer supported), 2-wave audience research (survey + 12-20 depth interviews), competitor map of 14 titles, positioning matrix, anti-positioning statement, 3 message pillars, tagline testing, brand voice guidelines, visual identity brief, 3-tier revenue forecast (base/bull/bear), live service revenue model (cosmetic-only), LTV by segment, pricing strategy (base game + cosmetic store), marketing budget (launch + ongoing), channel strategy (paid + organic), influencer tiering (40 creators across 3 tiers), launch funnel definition, wishlist targets at 6/3/1 month, wishlist campaign roadmap, Steam page strategy (copy + tags), Steam Next Fest plan, 3 trailer briefs, press release schedule, localisation plan (9 languages recommended), region strategy, awards strategy, KPI definitions, crisis comms plan, marketing playbook compilation. BD sub-feature: 40-publisher longlist, fit scoring to shortlist of 10, pitch deck, data room, outreach campaign, term sheet template with red lines, publish-vs-self-publish decision framework, platform onboarding (Steam, Epic, GOG, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch 2).

## Decisions / Insights

- Glen decided: Explorer is primary persona; Killer is "supported not primary" to bound PvP design decisions
- Glen decided: anti-positioning statement (what the game is NOT) is as important as positioning
- Glen observed: Steam Next Fest is the single biggest wishlist driver outside paid acquisition
- Glen observed: "cosy MMO" is becoming a category; genre flag choice (cosy vs exploration RPG) is strategic
- Glen decided: term sheet red lines (recoupment caps, IP retention, sequel rights, key person) must be documented before any publisher outreach
- Glen decided: all outbound external messages require Glen's approval

## Context

Built as part of Couch Heroes production backlog (build_inputs/) for the April 2026 offsite. Studio is ~55 employees UK+Greece, pre-production MMORPG with F2P cosmetic monetisation.
