## Purpose

NBI Operations is the ongoing business management project that keeps NBI Consulting (NBI Analytics Ltd / National Business Innovations LLC) running day to day. This is not a time-bounded project with a delivery date — it is a perpetual operational system. Its purpose is to ensure Glen is never the bottleneck for routine operations, that no client commitment falls through the cracks, that the BD pipeline is actively managed, that financial health is visible and tracked, and that weekly reporting surfaces problems before they become crises.

Glen is stretched across 4-5 clients simultaneously (Couch Heroes as primary time commitment, Lighthouse Studios on contract oversight, Sarge Universe in active pre-funding preparation, Blizzard via Tom, and Goals Studio with a follow-up now overdue). He cannot manually track all of this. NBI Operations is the system that handles what would otherwise fall through the gaps.

## Scope

### In scope

**Client delivery oversight:**
- Tracking status of all active client engagements: Couch Heroes, Lighthouse Studios, Sarge Universe, Blizzard (Tom-managed), Goals Studio
- Monitoring deliverable deadlines and flagging anything overdue or at risk
- Confirming the Lighthouse embedded team (Amir, Ruan, Stavros) is on track against the 3-year contract — particularly ahead of the regular data science & analytics hour meetings
- Tracking the Sarge Universe pre-funding timeline (pitch deck, DD deck, financial plan — critical w/c 6 April deadline)
- Flagging overdue follow-ups (e.g. Goals Studio / Jonas Rundberg — follow-up was committed post-GDC, now overdue as of 28 March 2026)

**BD pipeline management:**
- Maintaining visibility of all active leads, warm contacts, and pipeline opportunities
- Tracking Glen's lead follow-up commitments and surfacing overdue ones
- Supporting CMO / Head of BD Agent with outreach drafts and pipeline hygiene
- Monitoring new inbound enquiries (Info@NBI-Consulting.com, website contact form, LinkedIn)
- Tracking the investor outreach pipeline for Sarge Universe (Jackson at RMT, Riley Graebner at Magna Capital, Binni at Behold Ventures, Manjit Dawe at TDR Capital — emails targeted by end of w/c 6 April 2026)
- Supporting Tom's human capital practice BD efforts (no contracts generated to date — this is a known gap)

**Financial tracking:**
- Monthly visibility of payroll costs against revenue (currently £52,117/month UK payroll)
- Tracking revenue by client: Couch Heroes (primary), Lighthouse Studios (3-year embedded contract), and any others
- Monitoring the NSI transition financial risk: when NSI winds down, Tom (£200K), Bryan (£200K), Jeff (£150K potential), and Jessica (£70K potential) move onto NBI payroll — estimated additional £620K/year, bringing total from £625K to ~£1.245M. This is the single largest financial planning risk and must be tracked
- Working with CFO Agent and Bryan Rasmussen (human CFO) to maintain QuickBooks accuracy
- Flagging any months where revenue does not cover payroll
- Devin Rieger's SalarySage work — tracking whether this is generating revenue or remains an internal project

**Weekly reporting:**
- A weekly status report delivered to Glen every Monday morning (or the first active day of the week)
- Report covers: client delivery status, BD pipeline status, financial snapshot, overdue actions, upcoming deadlines in the next 7 days, and any escalated risks
- Report format: bullet points with narrative detail. Not a wall of text, not a one-line list — each item has enough context to act on without Glen needing to dig further
- Glen does not want to be asked whether he wants the report — it is delivered proactively

**Kali Pryer (Producer) coordination:**
- Kali works as Producer (£4,500/month), primarily on internal tracking, accounts, and cross-practice support
- COO Agent supports Kali's producer work with drafts, scheduling assistance, and milestone tracking
- Track any accounts or invoice-related items Kali is managing

**Patrice (HR / General Admin) coordination:**
- Patrice handles general administration across the full business (£4,000/month)
- Any HR or admin tasks that arise (hiring processes, onboarding, document requests) flow through Patrice with agent support for drafting and tracking

### Out of scope

- Technical product development for Playsage, SalarySage, or the NBI website (those are separate product projects)
- Client delivery of consulting work itself — NBI Operations tracks and reports on delivery, but does not perform the consulting analysis
- Glen's personal schedule and calendar (handled via Google Calendar / Granola in Glen's personal CoWork setup)
- The NSI business itself (NSI is being wound down / sold; NBI Operations tracks the transition impact on NBI, not NSI's operations)
- Tom Rieger's human capital practice management in detail (Tom runs his own practice; NBI Operations tracks pipeline outcomes and financial impact, not Tom's day-to-day work)
